The whiskey production should be kosher but I know many Whiskeys are matured in Ex-Sherried & ex-Port casks. These are fortified wines and therefore may not be Kosher.
I'm not sure what ratio of Sherry Casks are used in Oowers but I know they're used.

'KELLAN' Irish whiskey which was a Cooley whiskey launched around 2008 and
which was exclusive to the U.S. market was the first whiskey that was Kosher
certified --- that is what it was advertised as at the time. Obviously a lot of other
brands have gone for Kosher certification since then.
