I remember an old Players and Jameson one of similar style for around €20 each at an auction a while ago.they also had a metal wire surround. I though it was cheap and should have bidded. I would guess at €30/40 on a good day. It could be from the 50's or 60's but it's over 40 years old because the whiskey doesn't have an E. That came in in the early 70's,but i stand to be corrected. It's one for the whiskyauctions.com. The old bottles get around €100 so you'd never know someone may want a set.
