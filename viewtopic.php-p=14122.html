Re: allman and co whisky

by TheWhiskeyBro » Fri Jun 22, 2012 4:24 pm

Hi there the major difficulty you have is that the seal is gone, this means from a collectors point of view the value is greatly diminished as you cannot prove the contents are original. For example if you brought it to a specialist retailer they probably would not make you an offer. The value really is whatever you can get, it is only a guess but you could get as little as €50 or as much as €200 if someone wanted to take the risk that contents were original and unspoilt.

Just one view, other may agree or disagree.
