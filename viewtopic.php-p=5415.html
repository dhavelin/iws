Hi guys, check these out

http://www.silversprings.ie/whiskey/wol ... index.html

http://www.silversprings.ie/whiskey/hor ... index.html

Two new whiskies, targeted mainly at the US export market, one a blend and one a 12 year old, it is a Clonmel based company (Tullamore Dew has a local competitor ), I'm presuming they are from the Cooley stable until I hear otherwise. I like the names, always thought a village like 'Horse and Jockey' deserved to have a whiskey named after it.

Still at the pre-launch phase I imagine.

See company profile here http://www.silversprings.ie/about.html

Registered as a company in September 2009 and website seems to be c. March 2010
