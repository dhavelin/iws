Are you sure about that code Birdman?

As The Whiskey Bro said L4 is 2004 or 2014, the next 3 numbers are the date ***/365 or 366 in this case of 2004. The next digit should be 3. That was bottled in House Of Donohue in Waterford. 1- Fox & Geese, 2- Belfast & 3- HOD. The next are the batch numbers.

I think the Coses were before 2006. The 2005 Redbreast 15 have them etched in the glass and I've a 2004 Jameson 12 also etched in the glass.
Nowadays it's found on the rear label.

Amazingly my bottle was filled the same day but a little later on.

The code is L4 218 ? 145. The ? is slightly damaged and isn't clearly visible. I expected it to be 3 but I've a 2005 bottle which is L5 326 5 288. I'm guessing the 1,2,3 were added after.
I've an 2011 and it's L1 234 3 1278 (time)
It has 22/08/11 under it.

As you can see the earlier codes have 8 digits while the later has 9.

It's a 2004 bottle. My 2004 and 2005 came with plinths.

The Redbreasts of the same era were also House Of Donohue and the 5th (of 8 too)digit of the code is also 3.
