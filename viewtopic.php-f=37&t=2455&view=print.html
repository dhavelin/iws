Just catching up this weekend on events over the last week or so.

One of the nice side benefits of attending the Google Hangout with the Midleton Whiskey Masters a week ago was the wonderful "Aprés Party" with fellow IWS members from Dublin, Cork, Galway and the South East afterwards.

It was great to finally meet so many IWS Irish Whiskey Legends in person who previously I had only chatted to online - here or on other Irish Whiskey related Social Media.

I think we coined a term "Irish Whiskey Clan" that night?

What a marvelous and eclectic group of Irish Whiskey aficionados. Young and Old, Single Malt and Pot Still, Boys and Girls, Irish and Honorary Irish, but all great characters!

Best of all was the tremendous sense of Fun and Passion for Irish Whiskey and Life in general.

Great end to a great event Guys!

Kind regards,

Stuart
