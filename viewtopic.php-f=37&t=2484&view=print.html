The Irish Whiskey Society visit Kilbeggan -

Thanks to all at Beam Suntory and Kilbeggan Distilling Company. We met the distiller Paudge. Tour guide Carol Harrington gave an excellent tour of the visitor centre/museum and warehouses, followed by a sample of new spirit and a tasting of their range - Kilbeggan, Kilbeggan 8 year Old, Tyrconnell and Connemara. But it did not end there, John Cashman global brand ambassador provided us with a number of samples from the casks. We have seen the 21 year old Kilbeggan and the 22 year old Connemara releases in the past year. We look forward to seeing some more from them going forward. Have you tried any of their whiskeys? Let us know what you think of them!
