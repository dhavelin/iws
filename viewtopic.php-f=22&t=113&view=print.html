Irish whiskey is not just for St Patrick's Day

Posted: Mon May 11, 2009 3:22 pm
by DavidH
Here's an interesting graphic:

Search trends for "Irish Whiskey", "Scotch Whisky"

It shows the search volume for each term on Google's servers over the last few years. There is a spike of interest before Christmas for both Irish and Scotch but also a massive spike for Irish alone around St Patrick's Day.

It's useful, perhaps, for the society in its promotional activities - there is search traffic to be captured every March. But we might also view it as a challenge - to encourage people to think about Irish whiskey at other times of the year too.
