Newsflash: Power's Whiskey Goes Retro

Posted: Sun Nov 07, 2010 3:36 pm
by TheWhiskeyBro
I'm really excited and thrilled at this latest development...

http://www.powerswhiskey.ie/powers-casks.html

The Stag's Head & Keoghs in Dublin and Bridies in Kilkenny in a truly innovative departure are now serving Power's from the cask.

I have to give a big to IDL for this nod to our past which also shows that the marketing guys can leverage on tradition to produce a win/win situation.

Must pop in to the Stag's head to do a Powers head-to-head, straight from the bottle vs straight from the cask (I presume both are at 40%).

Certainly a bit of a novelty but not to be dismissed out of hand.

I know this is not unheard of in other countries but still to be commended.

What does everyone else think?
