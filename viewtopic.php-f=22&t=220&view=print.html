I don't know any off licences with a great range of whisk(e)y. Sainsburys has a reasonable whisk(e)y range, including an own brand Irish single malt made by Cooley.

Direct Wine Shipments in Belfast is the best off licence in the North that I know. Terrific range of wine.

Nicholsons in Downpatrick is also good.

Long's supermarket in Derry, now a SuperValu, sometimes gets bottles from Gordon and McPhail, but I don't know if they've got any lately. Weirdly enough, I've seen G&M bottlings in a small off licence in Lisnaskea.

Winemark and Curleys are the biggest local off licence chains but I haven't ever seen a great range of whiskeys in them. Winemark in the Buttercrane, Newry, had Old Comber in the 90s, for £30, but foolishly I didn't buy it...
