My father is from Kilkenny and my Children are from Galway so a hard one for me to choose but went for the Tribes men on the same pretence as JohnM. Obviously we can't hand out titles as we see fit but I think Galway have shown that they have the appetite as well as the skill to match Kilkenny. Yesterdays match was not a brilliant match but it was no where near a bad match either. Basically a great contest played both hard and passionate. It certainly was an edgy game which added to the whole excitement of the game.

I actually thought the ref had an okay game compared to say the Kilkenny Tipp match which turned into a circus and all because the ref was letting the game "flow". The ref pulled up on a good few things which I thought were not frees but on replay were clearly right decisions. The Cats could have been forgiven for thinking he was giving everything against them in the first half but but it is easy to forget that Galway were pretty much first to the ball every time in the first half and such play inevitably leads to the opposition committing more fouls as they try harder to get a foot hold into the game. Fair play to Kilkenny though as they did not capitulate like another team could have and kept Galway with in distance and the second half we saw King Henry take centre stage and it was a wonderful battle between the two marks men. I'd love to see him get a 9th but I think he should wait till next year

Great game looking forward to the 30th also
