Posted: Mon Feb 09, 2015 5:17 pm
by IrishWhiskeyChaser
Got mine too .... arrived as if it had been chilled is was so cold but that is just the week that is in it.

Anyway could not resost so cracked it open at lunch time (I know very bold but really only a tiny bit ..... the advantages of working at home )

As I said it was quite cold so looking forward to a warmer drop later.

Anyway

Nose: Closely described on the label I get big sweet Raisin and this earthyness not quite matching the mustiness for some older expressions but a welcome addition too with a rich sherryness trying to burst through the dried fruit but not quite

Mouth Feel is very thick and smooth and buttery

Taste: big round and lip smacking gets you right in the cheeks with a nice pinch of spice in there too but the sweetness is still to the fore.

Finish: buttery butterscotch drying quickly (gin like) with a noticable lick of wood at the end.

I really need to revisit as the sample was really chilled but like a child I could not wait.

This for me does not dissapoint ... however not the sherry monster I was expecting or hoping for (maybe it will be more pronounced when at room temp) but a wonderful expression at the same time.

I'll pop back tomorrow and let ye know if anything has changed in the next sample
