April Tasting Bow Street Thu 28 Apr 6.30-9pm

by TheWhiskeyBro » Wed Apr 20, 2011 12:29 am

Members tasting of exclusive Bow Street/Midleton Distillery Reserve bottlings past and present hosted by Liam Donegan, Distiller, IDL.

Chat and introduction to major developments in the world of Single Pot Still (formerly PPS).

Launch by Ken Mawhinney of the IWS sponsored limited edition hardback pamphlet on a historic view of Irish Whiskey.

Presentation of antique bottle of pre-prohibition bottle of Jameson 5 Star to the Distillery Museum.

All bookings must be fully paid prior to the event in order to confirm attendance.

18.30 - 19.00: Arrival and finger food canapes.
19.00 Welcome note and whiskey tasting
19.45 General comments, interesting news, and surprise whiskey to taste.
20.15 Launch of pamphlet.
20.25 Presentation of 5* Jameson
20.45 - 21.00 departure.

Limited places for members so please book early at http://bookwhen.com/iws (click on header page above to get to main menu for booking)

This is sure to be a memorable evening and another landmark event for the Irish Whiskey Society, not to be missed.
