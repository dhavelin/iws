by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Wed Oct 10, 2012 12:03 pm

we really need to see pictures of any bottles you have.

In regards to the Redbreast, 3-400euro is a retail price and you will be very unlikely to achieve that in the open market.

The 10 year old's will be slightly more rare than the 12 but in saying that they all pop up fairly regularly so not overly rare.

can't really see these going for over 200 Euro

Auction price for such bottles will be between 100-180 Euro so that is where you are looking at.

However if anyone has evidence to the contrary please post.
