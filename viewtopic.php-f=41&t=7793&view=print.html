Hi Carole,

It's an independent bottling label. Before Irish Distillers formed in the 60's, there were many independent bottlers. They simply bought the spirit in bulk, often by cask & bottles it themselves. This was probably a Jameson although it doesn't say on the label. The font & design looks like an old Jameson label anyway.
