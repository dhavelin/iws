Locke's 8yo Single Malt (Peated)

Posted: Tue May 03, 2011 4:34 pm

Nose: Light smooth sweet malt, Pear Drops, Orange squash

Taste: Boiled Sweets, pear drops again, Apricot. And a quick spice b

Finish: Dusty and dry spice, White pepper, anestetic Smoke(light)

Comment: Not bad and quite lightly peated compared to the Connemara. A reall easy starter for those wantingb to get into peated whiskies and a very drinkable and enjoyable malt.
