Welcome to the Irish Whiskey Society forum

We are delighted that you have taken the time to visit our forum and we hope you enjoy it. Please feel free to register to be part of a great and friendly community. This forum has been set up and paid for by a group of like minded individuals to discuss all kinds of whiskey, and Irish whiskey in particular. The forum is a free resource for all to enjoy but please treat it with respect. We ask you, therefore, to please familiarise yourself with the rules of the forum before posting.

Forum Rules

This forum has been set up and paid for by The Irish Whiskey Society. While we want it to be easygoing as well as educational we do have to create rules to protect the integrity of the Forum. Please abide by the rules and together we can create an enjoyable, informative forum.
  • The content of this forum is alcohol related therefore we ask you to be of the required legal drinking age in your country of residence.

    Please drink sensibly

    For more information on alcohol and the risks it poses visit
    Drinkaware.ie.JPG (11.7 KiB) Viewed 5949 times


  • Please post in the relevant forum. Misplaced posts may be moved or deleted otherwise.
  • Please take care with spelling, punctuation and grammar by proofreading your post before submission. Also please avoid the use of text speak/spelling. This is respectful to other users of the forum. It also helps other users when searching for posts on, say, "Midleton" (one 'D'). Your browser can be set to spell check automatically.
  • Healthy debate and argument is encouraged and welcomed, just remember not to over step the mark. Be prepared to agree to disagree before arguments get out of hand or overly heated. Rude or aggressive behaviour will not be tolerated.
  • No trolling or flaming. Such posts will be deleted.
  • Be respectful of others' opinions and backgrounds. Political, racial, religious, sexist or any other type of abuse will not be tolerated and may lead to your account being disabled.
  • No foul or defamatory language.
  • No self appointed moderating. Please report any unsavoury behaviour immediately. Use the report button (!) at the top of the relevant post and the moderators will deal with it.
  • The moderators' decisions are final whether the community considers them right or wrong. Any objections need to be forwarded to moderators and not published on the forum.
  • We encourage feedback and freely invite submissions of questions or concerns. However public posts debating these rules and/or moderators' enforcement of such will be removed without comment.
  • No Spam, pornography, or posts infringing copyright.
  • No commercial posts or links that are promoting your own or another’s business unless specific to the forum is allowed.
  • Any unconventional, nonsensical or lewd user-names may be deleted. If you account is deleted and you feel wrongly so please contact us and we can investigate the issue.
  • Please do not overload posts or your signature with graphics. Remember, not everyone has as much bandwidth as you.
  • Constant offenders may have their account disabled.
  • This site is run as a free and open forum but remains the sole property of ‘The Irish Whiskey Society’. All rights and decisions remain with the owners.
