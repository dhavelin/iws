Leitrim Bar, Seafront, Wicklow town have a good selection of Irish Whiskeys and a few goodone of Scotch

prices are really good too

they haven't got any from Cooley (but i informed John Cashman to do something about that) but have the full selection of Jameson and Bushmills range

friendly staff, good food, a good bar to go to, if you are in Wicklow town
Happiness is having a rare steak,a bottle of whiskey, and a dog to eat the rare steak!!!
