Hi all,
I am here a while now and have researched a lot on this forum (countless hours of reading) and have learnt a lot!

Firstly apologies if i should not have started a new thread here but decided was best way to introduce myself!

From a financial point of view investing in Irish whiskey maybe safe but for sure not lucrative, however it is my way of inheriting history and passing on our drink one day. I do like a dram and have enjoyed bottles such as a red breast 12 amongst a scotch or two.

As regards collecting whiskey Scotch is number one but to me I have no interest personally in Scotch.

So i have received great advice from people such as JohnM and Irishwhiskeychassr and kurtbenoit but all advice and offers had to be put on hold until i finished my professional exams and could spend the time researching before committing. There was also the issue of putting the limited funds i held into education or whiskey and as much as i love it that choice had to be taken.

Now that i am finished exams, i have finally kick started the collection as follows;-
