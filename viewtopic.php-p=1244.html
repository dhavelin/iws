by PureDrop » Thu Sep 17, 2009 10:08 pm

The following are details on our planned tasting for October 1st:
Venue: Brookes Hotel, Drury St., Dublin 2
Time: 19:30

Tasting:

Tyrconnell Single Malt (no age statement, Cooley)
Locke's "Grand Crew" Single Malt, Single Cask No. 700, 9yo,
Locke's "Grand Crew" Single Malt, Single Cask No. 696, 9yo,
Tyrconnell 10yo Madeira Finish
Tyrconnell 10yo Sherry Finish
Tyrconnell 10yo Port Finish

6bottles1.jpg (11.04 KiB) Viewed 26156 times

This is a chance to taste the first two casks selected by the society.
See thread hereand the Grand Crew website here.

With the three Tyrconnell finishes, we'll be able too to examine the impact of various finishing casks on the malt.
To help the process, we'll distribute some "nosing" samples of each of: Oloroso Sherry, Malmsey Madeira and LBV Port.

See you there.
NOTE: Any changes to the event (whiskies or timings) wil be indicated on this post - check back occasionally!
