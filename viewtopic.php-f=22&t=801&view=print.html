If you had three wishes and could choose to bottle a whiskey, one from each of the three main distilleries (Bushmills, Cooley/Kilbeggan & Midleton) what would you like to see and why.

The only condition is the distillery in question has to be able to provide your bottling either now or in the future.

Now fire away and let your imagination run riot and your taste buds salivate at the thought.... if only!

If I Had Three Wishes... !

Posted: Thu Aug 12, 2010 6:50 pm
by TheWhiskeyBro
(1) From Bushmills : The Blackbush 8 Plus Aged Blend @ 46%

As Blackbush is the 'go to' whiskey for many people, I would love to know what an older blend (say 8-10-12yo with mature grain) in a similar style would taste like particularly at 46% abv. Aagh imagine those luscious sherry tones at a tongue coating 46% abv, it could be stupendous.

(2) From Cooley/Kilbeggan : The Greenore Extra Mature 18 Year Old @ 43%

Aged grain from the cask, with mouthfuls of banana and exotic fruit, it could be wonderful even better as a single cask. And we know how great an old grain whiskey can be.

(3) From Midleton : The Powers Special 15 Year Old @ 43%

The Irishman's Irish whiskey, a traditional style high PPS content (say 80%) blend could evoke memories of your grandfather. A cracking dry, flinty and spicy blend, hints of peaches and pair, with a touch of the older style heavy pot still mouth-feel, now would that be a wonderful thing. I can just see it now, walking into the bar, he shouts, A 'Power's Special' please if you don't mind!
