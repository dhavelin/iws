the original shamrock whiskey is an old and probably extinct bottling from the long dead Connswater distillery in Belfast, but you still see old decorative adds for it here and there. If its a contemporary whiskey, its probably one of Cooley's side project blends and, to be honest, if you're going to go to the trouble of hunting down a whiskey specifically and paying for shipping to vermont, you could probably do much better
