Hi folks,

I'm new, so please be gentle. I'm Tim Forbes and I work for The Whisky Exchange - I recognise a few of your user names / avatars from the less specialised forum I frequent more regularly

Don't worry, I'm not here to plug anything, but to ask for your help - I'm attempting to upgrade our St. Patrick's Day feature for next month. I spent most of yesterday wading through various topics on here (and links therefrom) to try and corral together the latest on the very fast developments of the last year or so, but I still have a few questions. I love Irish whiskey, but I'm only a dabbler compared to most of you folk, so I thought I'd throw them out there in the hope of taking advantage of your far more in-depth knowledge:

Firstly, I've read about West Cork Distillers’ infusion and maturation etc, but not much about an actual distillery apart from a passing mention in an Independent article – are they actually distilling there, and if they are, although most of their products are wood-matured savoury brown beverages at the moment are they likely to be whiskey in the future? Or are they buying in spirit from elsewhere and doing stuff to it? I couldn't find any references to whiskey anywhere on their website.

Secondly, I know Dingle and Alltech / Carlow are already distilling, but though I’ve seen mention on here of up to 14 possible new distilleries, I couldn’t find them all. Are there any other mooted distillery projects worth mentioning (in what is likely to be just a list in a very brief overview on the website) apart from the following:

Tullamore
Slane Castle
Derry
Horse Island
TWC, Dublin
Crumlin Rd
Feckin, Kircubbin

Any input much appreciated.

Many thanks,

Tim
