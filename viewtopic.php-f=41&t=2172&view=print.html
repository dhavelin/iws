by Raven
Millars of Thomas St. only finally closed in the early 80's as far as I know.
They used to bottle it as Millar's Black Label at one time. The label has been
re-incarnated as Millar's Special Reserve --- used the Black Label term on the
early re-incarnated bottles but had to change due to a threat from a Scottish
Distiller that makes another well known 'Black Label' . Like all bonders they would
have got casks of 10 year old and bottled it themselves prior to their supply being
cut off in the late 60's early 70's. The original Millar's Black Label blend continued
till the early 80's. Bottle most likely from last period (Late 60's/early 70's)
