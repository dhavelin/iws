Connemara Turf Mór relaunch!

Posted: Sat Nov 19, 2016 6:33 pm
by samuelkarlsson
Hey friends! Just wanted to pop by and let you all know that Connemara Turf Mór is being relaunched!

The first version of heavily peated (50ppm) Turf Mór was released back in 2010 and held 58,2% ABV, this version however holds 46% ABV and is released for selected airports within european travel retail. This is the first release of a new expression of Connemara in over two years, so really looking forward to trying it!
