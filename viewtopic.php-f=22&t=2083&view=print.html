Whisky Magazine Reviews

Posted: Wed Dec 18, 2013 11:04 pm
by Good Whiskey Hunting
My latest edition of Whisky Magazine has arrived.
This issue has tasting notes for Powers Gold, Powers signature reserve and Redbreast 21.
The Powers Gold is the new version of Powers NAS blend available outside Ireland I believe. It's 43.2% ABV.
The tasters were Annabel (A)and Martine(M).
Here's what they gave them.

Powers Gold
A; 7.4 M;7.7

Powers Signature Release
A; 6.5 M;7.8

Redbreast 21
A;6.6 M;9

I think Annabel may not be a Pot Still fan. How will we convince her otherwise?
