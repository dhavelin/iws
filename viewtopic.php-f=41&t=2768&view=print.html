Lovely label all right, I'm a big fan of Jim's art work however what I think you have there is a liqueur and not whiskey.

The vol states 20% which is not legal to be called whiskey but the vol is so low it would suggest something along the line of a liqueur though.

The 750ml also means it was possibly made for the US market but then again it was back in '88 so we could still have been using the 75cl size but I can't remember of hand.

So unfortunately I do not think there is much collectablity in it for a whiskey collector and is more of a curiosity, but still maybe €100 but that is a complete stab in the dark but surely worth that.

Where the real gold may lie is in the 25year old Jameson. Is it a Jameson or a Midleton. If a Midleton 25yo possibly worth anything from €1000-1500 in the open market, just to be aware a retailer will be looking for 2500-3000 for it but do not let those figures cloud your judgement as colectors will not pay retail for a bottle such as this especially when they cost less than €400 less than 10 years ago. But that one came out in the late 90's Millenium period. If it is a Jameson we would need to see a picture.
