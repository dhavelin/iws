A nice dash of Coke would probably make it palatable ...

Forgot to mention that I got a couple of Bushmills 1608 400th anniversary in Adli when they were in store a week or so ago.

Must admit I'm very partial to this release and prefer it over the 16yo. So I was delighted to get the opportunity to get it at €60 a pop. Even though after buying it I realised Ally has it for €64.99 so there was no major panic if I missed the Aldi offer.

This is a blend but barely as it has over 95% malt content, crystal malt to be precise. Colum Egan was telling me that they had hoped to bottle it as a single malt but he found that putting in a tiny amount of grain enhanced it perfectly. I'm not complaining ... and I admire him for doing it as selling this as a single malt may have given it more kudos to many buyers.
