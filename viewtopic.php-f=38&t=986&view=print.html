Re: Congratulations Malt-teaser

Posted: Thu Dec 23, 2010 10:50 am
by Malt-Teaser
Without a shadow of a doubt the Port Ellen.
In fact this is the most outstanding whisk(e)y I have tried in 2010, head and shoulders above any other.
I don't usually score whiskies on a day to day basis, but if I did this would be 92-93 which is a great score as I'm rather conservative in my scoring.

It's a single cask Port Ellen bottled by "Old Bothwell", cask No.2039 and what made it special was the variety of flavours and nuances which I have never previously found in the PEs that I tried.
I'm planning to put this into my Dram-atics "Advent-urous" series on Christmas Day and into my Port Ellen tasting notes sometime next week.

My thanks to Oliver who brought this one along as something special with which to celebrate our good news.
