There's a bridge to Skye from the Kyle of Lochalsh these days, but I still prefer the ferry from Mallaig (I just like ferries). Mull needs a ferry (45 mins) from Oban. Both islands are spectacularly beautiful, but very different. I'll try to post some photos soon.

I did get a few bottles when I was away. There were a couple of bottles of the Iona malt (which is a vatting of Tobermory and Ledaig), also a bottle of 7yo Ledaig. My favourite buy was a bottle of the 30th Anniversary Poit Dubh, from PrÃ ban na Linne, the Gaelic Whisky Company on Skye. It's a 36yo vatting and is very nice indeed. Poit Dubh 30th Anniversary Malt
