so i've been thinking over this for a little while and i might be able to help start something in San Francisco. There's a huge whiskey culture here, although most of it revolves around Scotch and Rye but i'm well connected to it (i work for Diageo, teach the history of whiskey class here at berkeley, and lecture at Bourbon and Branch's beverage academy program in the city centre) so i could easily coordinate something and probably talk my boss into donating some bushmills malts to an opening tasting. there's also a large Irish ex-pat community over here that i have at least a little bit of connection to through the collegiate GAA.
