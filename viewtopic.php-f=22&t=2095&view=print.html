Whiskey App for Windows Phone

Posted: Mon Jan 06, 2014 5:51 pm
by IrishWhiskeyChaser
A shout out to those of you who have windows phones.

A student from DCU has created an app for Windows phone only. This is basically part of her Degree so nobody complain about lack of availability on other platforms

Anyway most of the content is Jameson based with a basic list of a 4 or five Jamesons with tasting notes. A cocktail part again seems to be based on Jameson and some Jameson social media.

However there is also a part called Absorb which is basically the news area. This is actually quite interesting and covers all spectrums of the irish Whiskey industry so well worth a download for that alone. A smooth interface and not glitches so far so over all a nice app and it's free so no harm supporting it.
