by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Sun Jan 08, 2012 9:34 pm

These were release in the early 70's and possibly the pre-cursor to today's Midleton VR which is released anually.

I'm surprised at the IDL offer as I know they have versions of this now ... when was this offer made?

Up to 3 years ago these were as rare as hens teeth however these are more common that they used to be now. At their height you may have got 3-400Euro but that would have been for a good example, now not sure of value. However your example the label is in very poor condition thus possibly only worth €100-150.
