Cooley not supplying whiskey to own brands

Posted: Thu Apr 12, 2012 9:38 am
by Stephen
Article in today's indo

http://www.independent.ie/business/iris ... 78847.html

Cooley is cutting the supply of casks to independent bottlers, shows an intent to lay down casks and produce more higher age statements. I would see this as the end for a lot of the supermarket own brand bottlings of Irish Whiskey

Re: Cooley not supplying whiskey to own brands

Posted: Thu Apr 12, 2012 10:55 am
by DavidH
There's a business lesson there: get it in writing. Knappogue Castle, Irishman, Tullamore Dew, etc show you can hang in there with a good contract. It will be interesting to see who survives in the extended Cooley family.

More generally though, this is very positive for the quality of Irish whiskey. And it's another nudge for independents to set up their own distillery.
