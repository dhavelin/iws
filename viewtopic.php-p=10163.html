Whiskey Week

by Michael Foggarty » Sun Apr 24, 2011 1:56 pm

Here are the details of "Whiskey Week" the week leading up to WhiskyLive.

Monday 9th May, Rob Allanson(Editor of Whisky Mag) will conduct a tasting at The Old Bushmills distillery. Local consumer event. Duncan Taylor comes to Ireland launch night at L. Mulligan. Grocer,consumer/trade/press event.

Tuesday 10th May, Rob Allanson will take on Michael Foggarty in a Scotland v Ireland taste off event at L. Mulligan. Grocer. 7.30 pm start, consumer/trade/press event.

Wednesday 11th May, Rob Allanson continues his journey around Ireland with a visit to Kilbeggan Distillery, he will also hold a tasting in the distillery on that day.

Thursday 12th May, Rob Allanson hosts lunchtime tasting at Tullamore Heritage centre (consumer event). Lord Mayors press photo shoot at The Mansion house at 5.30 pm. Followed by a repeat of the Scotland v Ireland taste off which will be held at Brooks at 7.30 pm (corporate/trade/press event)

Friday 13th May, Michael Foggarty and Rob Allanson to host lunchtime bartender training course venue TBC, specialising in tasting and serving techniques (much needed in many of Dublin's bars).

Saturday 14th, Lord Mayor to open WhiskyLive Dublin. 2 p.m. - 7 p.m. During the event there will be an auction of the "All Ireland Blend", 1 of the 10 bottles created will be auctioned off for the Japanese Red Cross.

Various bar across Dublin city will also be offering special priced whiskey for the week and impromptu tastings from Brand Ambassadors/ distillery managers.

Your probably wondering what the All Ireland Blend is. Myself and Rob along with distillers/blenders from those from the 4 distilleries will create a blend to be bottled in a crystal decanter. The bottles will only be available to 1 for each disitllery, 1 for BMW(sponsoring Rob's bike to travel Ireland) 1 for the Mayor, 1 for Auction, 1 for the Publisher of WhiskyMagazine and 1 for me and Rob.
