Cadenhead Cooley 16yo World Whiskies 1992 (2008)

Posted: Tue May 03, 2011 4:59 pm
by IrishWhiskeyChaser
Nose: Citrus Alcohol, Iodine, dry earthy peat a bit closed

Taste: Strong flavours, and Carbolic Soap with out the suds

Finish: Smokey bacon, damp old leather boots

Comment: Despite the notes and how they sound but this is a cracking cooley peated with everything you want from a peat monster. If your into it peat you'll love it.
