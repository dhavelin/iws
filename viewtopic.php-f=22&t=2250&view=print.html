Re: Blackwater Distillery

Posted: Mon Jul 21, 2014 7:28 pm
by Blackwater
We will start production this year and we will start conservatively, I can't be more specific right now as one of the problems with setting up a business in rural Ireland is the shocking lack of basic infrastructure - from internet to waste water, everything is a battle and that is all impacting on R&D. But there's no point setting up a micro-distillery to replicate the offerings of the multi-nationals that control 99% of the spirit production in this country. The Scots are lashed to conformity by their sporran straps, while here in Ireland we have wonderfully open legislation and we're not exploiting it.
