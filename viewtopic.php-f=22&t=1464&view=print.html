There was at least 3 Midleton 30yos bottled.

One was from a 1969 cask released in time for the Millennium, I think. Not sure how many bottled of this was released.

One was single release of 1973 whiskey from at least 2 casks of which I know cask No 41421 & No 41422 which is a release of 1/800.

What would be a good price ... god only knows. Fairly rare and the reseller retail price in Celtic Whiskey Shop is €2,500

See here

So you'll have to find it on the open market to get it for less than that but they are hard to come by ...
