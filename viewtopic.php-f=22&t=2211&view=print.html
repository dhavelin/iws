A great night was had by all from the reports I've heard.

It was better than the last one which was great too. Hopefully it'll be better again next year. I didn't sign up for any masterclasses this time which meant I'd more time.

My first port of call was the Irishman. I was hoping they had the 2014 Cask Strength Writers Tears, they had and I wasn't disappointed. Another cracker.

I was very surprised by the Dunvilles blend. I will try get my hand on one.

The Jameson Caskmate was excellent, I hope they build on this.

I tried a sample of Teelings 30 year old.

Well done to all involved, especially Ally.
