Hi PJ,
The Cadenhead's value is hard to gauge. They seen to change with the weather. I've seen miniatures valued at over £100.

The Redbreast is from the 1980's so should be valued around €150+.

The Kilbeggan 15. They were around €75 when they were released in 2009 I think. I'm guessing they are worth at least €120 now. I can't rec any at auction recently. I know I drank mine.
