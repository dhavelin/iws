Re: Few bottles for sale

by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Tue Jul 24, 2012 1:46 pm

The Sale of Alcohol is prohibited on Ebay unless you have a licence to sell alcohol and registered your licence with Ebay.

However collectable bottles are permitted if advertised as so.

Ebay has a section covering it.

http://pages.ebay.co.uk/help/policies/alcohol.html


The sale of alcohol-related memorabilia is permitted, including packaging and unopened bottles, decanters, or limited edition cans that still contain alcohol.
Each of the following conditions must be met and stated word for word in the listing, in the same font size as the rest of the description:

    The value of the item is in the collectable container, not its contents.

    The container has not been opened and any incidental contents are not intended for consumption.

    The item is not available at any retail outlet, and the container has a value that substantially exceeds the current retail price of the alcohol in the container.

    The seller will take all appropriate steps to ensure that the buyer is of lawful age in the buyer’s and seller's jurisdiction (generally 18 years of age in the UK and Republic of Ireland).

    Buyers and sellers both ensure that the sale complies with all applicable laws and postage regulations.


We suggest copying the above bullet points and pasting this text directly into your listing.
