Hi Barry, as GWH said all these a very nice whiskeys in their own right and will become collectible but probably not for some years to come.

You might make 10 or 20 extra on say Celtic Cask ócht & Redbreast Mano a Lamh if you are lucky for find an eager buyer but if I were you I'd hang on to them for another few years.

The Powers Aviation is also still available in travel retail so no premium yet but another good one to hold on to.

However the 12 is still widely available and will not command a premium for a good few years after it goes out of circulation which is has not yet.

Open it and enjoy it and get yourself another down the line
