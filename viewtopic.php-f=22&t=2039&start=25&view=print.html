by JohnM
I kind of thought I'd be disappointed by this whiskey, but not at all.

Nose: Buttery almonds. Boozey raisins. Vanilla.

Taste: Rich, oily dried fruit. Anise. You kind of want to chew it. A little metalic. Sour oranges. Not much malt.

Long finish. Very long.

Kind of everything I want in a whiskey.
