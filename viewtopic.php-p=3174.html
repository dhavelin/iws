By the way a very useful site is www.interparcel.co.uk they will give you a quote for courier pick-ups anywhere in the U.K. and export to Ireland.

For example I've used them to pick up from the Cadenheads (i.e. Springbank) whisky shop as they had a 16yo cask strength peated Cooley. I've also used them to pick-up from Bladnoch for the forum bottlings . Each time it has worked out @£16 for up to 6 bottles (10kg).
