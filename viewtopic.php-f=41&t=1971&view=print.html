by TheWhiskeyBro
It was at most 10 year old Cooley Single Malt (distilling started Easter 1989) but most likely a younger whiskey. Preston's is usually a 10 year old Jameson blend although the website mentions pure pot still. It was bottled @ 43% from Cooley Cask #1319 on the 20 October 1999, yielding 330 bottles.

No reports of note on the contents apart from the dubious "Christmas Pudding" tasting notes on the original website. A collectible curiosity from yet another Millennium venture. I'd value it a €150-€200
