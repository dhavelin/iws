Re: Auction Watch

Posted: Sun Dec 01, 2013 2:07 pm
by Good Whiskey Hunting
If you get multiple bottles is spreads the cost of postage. If you were getting say the Paddy, a Tullamore and Tyrconnell say. The cost was €34 I think. Postage is €23 and handling is €9. That comes to €66. It may still be cheaper that the shops but it's not value.

I usually factor this in before I bid. I usually get a few bottles because if you get one it's €19+ €3 handling. I managed to get some nice older bottles and a few cheap one. I would have saved myself €50+ and got a few rare ones.

Other auctions charge commission, sometimes 16% and VAT on top and this can drive the costs up. If you need to get them posted than the cost is often not worth it.
I try take it in account at all auctions. It's worth while making a list before and give yourself a max price. It's sticking to this that's the problem.
