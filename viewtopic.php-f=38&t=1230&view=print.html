Has anyone ever read the wikipedia articles on "Irish Whiskey" or "Pure Pot Still" whiskey? They're absolutely terrible and littered with crap like "Pot still blends like greenspot vs single malts like bushmills." I suppose the PPS article shouldnt even exist as the names been changed for quite some time now but its been hijacked by editers who assume its an article about the theoretical use of a pot still at all and have gotten rid of any national or unmalted/malted mixture references. anyway, as i know the society is very interested in educating the public about irish whiskey and wikipedia is a hugely influential space, i thought i should bring it to your attention in case anyone has the time to go over there and do some clean up work.
