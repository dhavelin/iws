Hi David,

Afaik these whiskeys are produced by http://irishfiddlerwhiskey.com/ from casks previously acquired from Cooley a number of years ago.

Heide at the http://www.irelandwhiskeytrail.com seems to have the lowdown.

Apparently whiskey enthusiast Colm Redmond is behind this Galway whiskey, who knows he may even have aspirations for a distillery???

It is featured in Heidi's article here http://www.irelandwhiskeytrail.com/?pg=Zetland_Country_House_Hotel_Connemara.php

Martine Nouet, whiskey writer, consultant and food & whiskey matcher worked on the project for/with Colm.

Colm is the owner of the http://www.zetland.com/
