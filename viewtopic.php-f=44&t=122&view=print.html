by IrishWhiskeyChaser
In response to JC's chat about Greenspot I decided to post up my notes ... for what they are worth ???

Greenspot 40%

Nose: Honey'd Malt,Over ripe plum, light sweet Treacle, Citrus plus pleasant head clearing vapours (not quite vic's though .

Taste: Quite smooth initially but in no way light. Nice honey. However the smoothness is tempered with a slight metalic zing to give it a bit of kick.

Finish: Nice medium length with a bit of spice on the tip of the tongue with fruity Mint and a lovely dryness at the very end

Comment: Greenspot is an Irish institution which we are lucky to have and should have a protection notice drawn down for it. A winner for me.
