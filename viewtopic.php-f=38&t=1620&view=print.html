Was really looking forward to the Euro 2012 campaign and had great (if optimistic) hopes for Ireland until of course last night

I think we can book the plane home ....

Can't really say they deserved to win but the First Goal was very soft, second Goal stung of off-Side and a bit of a defensive disaster, third goal just finished us off totally. We seemed to have no fight against the Croat's and in all fairness to them they were all over us giving us very little chance to pass it around in the second half, closing down options very well. And after seeing Italy in good form against the mighty Spain I fear for the rest of the group stages. We may end up being the whipping boys which would be very sad ...
