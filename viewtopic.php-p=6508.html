That would be fine, is there any chance you could mail it to me please, along with IWC's?

The reason I ask for a mail is due to the latest business explosion here in Germany where firms of lawyers exist purely to trawl the internet and find sites using logos, pictures or graphics which they think may have been illegitimately 'borrowed'. They then send a mail to the website asking for proof of permission to use the graphic(s) and if none can be provided, they threaten to open a lawsuit along the lines of "Pay us €xxx or we will open a lawsuit and have you fined €xxxx".
For this reason I keep a copy of every e-mail that I receive with the logos as this is excellent proof of permission.

Crazy? Yes, but a 100% 'legal scam'.

They do the same with music or film downloading and 'sharing', even though the German Government has said they won't prosecute such misdemeanours any more, lawyers are opening private lawsuits and making rather good livings out of this and not on behalf of any intellectual property owners, just for themselves!

I recently read an article that at least one firm of lawyers in England thought this an excellent business plan and were looking to do the same there. Be careful, it may spread to Ireland too.

You can mail either to the contact address on the Emporium, or to my private address with which I registered here.
