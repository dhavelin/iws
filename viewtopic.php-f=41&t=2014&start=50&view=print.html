Personally I would not be happy with either particularly the 2 nd. One. Never came across Irish Whiskey from
A bonder etc. Coming in a similar type vessel. The top one is a jug of a type used up till the 1980's to bottle 'potcheen'
Which was done by Woodford Bourne here in Cork. I've a feeling that they could be the product of the Irish pub phenomenon
of the 1990's onward. In the end there was'nt enough bric a brac to go around to ship out to the various places where pubs we're opening and as a result they had to improvise and make up new stuff. Definitely have seen other stuff that arose in this way. As some pubs close up or get themed into the next new big craze then the old accessories end up on e-bay etc.

If you cannot physically handle/examine the item then the dictum of 'caveat emptor' definitely applies .
