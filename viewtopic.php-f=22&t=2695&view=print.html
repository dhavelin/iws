Re: Shipping whiskey across the EU

Posted: Wed Apr 13, 2016 9:37 am
by IrishWhiskeyChaser
Correct AnPost do ban the mailing of liquids and actually specifically mention alcohol as a banned substance to send through the post.

However I have always used AnPost for posting samples as they are easy to post and do not raise usually raise questions at the counter when posting.
Use small boxes as opposed to envelopes and pack well making sure they do not bang off each other.

It's the posting of full bottles that I would not go though AnPost as you have no cover if anything does go wrong.

For full bottles I've used ecoparcel.eu a few times with out any issue and although it is not the fastest service probably the cheapest.
