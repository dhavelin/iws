Re: I just finished a bottle of...

by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Mon Feb 05, 2018 11:43 am

Just emptied the bottle bin at the recycling bank. A few bottles now laid to rest over the last few months.

Chapter 7 13yo Sherry Single Cask @ 59.5%. I really love this so much so I have started a second and have a 3rd in the cellar.
Jameson 18yo, lovely stuff but I can't really justify the price so probably will not have another for a long while.
West Cork Dha Chasca, this is obviously a Cooley and is matured in Bodega Sherry casks & finished in double charred first fill Bourbon casks. Was hard for me to get past the young cooley style as I don't think cooley comes into it's own until well into it's late teen and early 20's. Not a bad effort though and the price point was very fair. West Cork are starting to get things going. Give them another few years and they might have a few winners.
Johnny Walker Gold Label. A wonderful blend from JW and highly recommended. Smooth & easy drinking but complex all the same and full of flavour.
Makers MArk one of my fave bourbons and sadly no replacement for the time being.
Clarkes Bourbon (Aldi) @ €15 a bottle this is not bad at all. Does for a quick nip with ice after a few beers.
Glen Marnoch Peated single Malt from Aldi. These were very cheap and well worth teh price. A bit young and fiery though but does the job. Often used it to blend with non peated Scotch and does a rgeat job of peating non peated whisky
