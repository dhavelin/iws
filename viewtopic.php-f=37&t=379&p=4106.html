I think the poll results, albeit from a small sample, are very interesting. Often with these things you get one or two clear leaders but in this case the preferences seem very spaced out. I don't know what it tells us but I can think of 3 possibilities:

(1) Sample too small (hopefully not the case as that would tell us nothing)
(2) Not enough variety in the Cooley range for people to establish clear favourites
(3) A very broad range of tastes are represented in this forum and that preferences are very subjective meaning you should beware of buying whiskey based on someone else's marking system in books etc.

Anyone any thoughts?
