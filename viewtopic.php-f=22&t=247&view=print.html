Lagavulin 16yo

Posted: Fri Aug 21, 2009 8:50 pm
by IrishWhiskeyChaser
Finished a lovely dinner of Steak & spuds washed down with porter

Anyway a bit bloated and as it's friday so decided to break out the whiskey earlier than normal.

Last drop from a bottle of Lagavulin 16yo which made a nice large one

This is an evergreen in my stock ... I actually opened another one a couple of months ago so that it was well seasoned when this one was finished

Anyway this whisky is one of the all time classics ... an islay which has heavy layers of smoke which is like having wafts of smoldering fires in your glass ... but in no way harsh ... actually very smooth, silky smooth with velvety rich sherry.

A stunning creation and one of my all time favourites.
