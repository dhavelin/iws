Just found a shop that beats that unbeatable price for Black Bush - the Bushmills Distillery shop!
They've been clearing stock with a rotating sale (that they failed to tell anyone about) up at the distillery. Before Xmas, they were selling the 16 yo at a markdown price, apparently.
Currently, it's the turn of Black Bush and the 1608 anniversary edition (the one with the fancy box and the crystal malt).
Two bottles of BB for £30 stg currently. That translates into under 17 euro, duty paid, per bottle. Worth it if you're filling the boot, but perhaps not worth driving 330 miles (Dublin-Bushmills rtn) for.
The deal on the 1608 is even better - currently £30 a bottle, down from £54 at the distillery. I don't think anywhere on the globe is going to beat that price, especially for a whiskey for which stocks are dwindling.
Normal Bush in the new bottle is actually more expensive than BB up there at the moment.
No price drops on the 21 yo, distillery edition or the 16 yo currently.
Another thing I heard is that the 1608 is actually a vatting of 12 yo to 16 yo whiskeys. Never realised there was such older stock going into it. Perhaps there wasn't initially, and they're stretching it out with some 16 yo single malt stock? I don't know.
