Hi All,

We are playing with the idea of an Irish Whiskey locator.

Just looking for comments and ideas if people have them.

It would be based on Google maps and the Idea is for it to be an Ireland map for the time being. We would need to get together a criteria for inclusion as we probably should only be including premises that are making an effort to have a proper whiskey range or doing their bit in promoting Irish Whiskey.

So get you thinking caps on and let us know what we should be doing.

Until then here is a link to a test map to give people an Idea of what we can create. Also if anybody can do some magic with google maps please feel free to volunteer your time. This is just a sample map so don't worry about the comment/descriptions etc as they were done on the fly.
