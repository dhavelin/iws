Oh My God, what a Bourbon.
I have finally found a whiskey that gives me a huge (but very smooth) hit of caramel, toffee and vanilla on the taste but also delivers a great lengthly smooth finish again with tons of vanilla, milk chocolate and toffee. It's full of flavour but not too overpowering. It's a full 53.5% abv

Superb wood with fruit and spice on the nose

I thought I had died and gone to whiskey heaven!

Can't wait to try the 20 year old
