Re: News of WhiskyLive

by Good Whiskey Hunting » Mon Oct 07, 2013 1:04 am

I didn't see anything from Cooley that I wasn't familiar with. I got a nice sample of the Kilbeggan 18. I assume they will have older in time too. I was distracted by some of the Beam bourbons.

The Redbreast 21 is excellent. The price is steep but if you were to compare it with say Jameson RVR it looked like good value. I know it's not like for like. I can only imagine the work that is involved in trying to balance the various pot still whiskeys in there.

On another note, my personal highlight was trying a sample of the Malaga component of Yellow Spot. What a beast of a whiskey! I think the particular one I tried was 14 year old.

I would like to have tried the Teeling's 26 year old but time was not on my side.

Allteck has some interesting products too. I really was surprised by their Bourbon. The beer was very nice too. I'm looking forward to see what they will have for us in the future.

The big disappointment was the absence of Bushmills, but sin scéal eile.
