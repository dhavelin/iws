Alberta Premium 25 year old 40%

Posted: Sat Apr 06, 2013 5:31 am
by Joel1802
Colour: Deep gold

Nose: Polished wood, Demerara sugar, vanilla, sweet, caramel, wintergreen, pepper, perfume

body: very light

Palate: Wintergreen, oak, more dark sugar, perfume, ginger, floral, vanilla

Finish: Spice, cinnamon?, bitter chocolate, oak creeps in.

A bit simple and one-dimensional like all 100% Rye. None the less it is very drinkable, enjoyable, and interesting.

87
