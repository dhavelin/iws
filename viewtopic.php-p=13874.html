Re: YELLOW SPOT "Our New - SPS Release - Is Coming Soon"

by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Mon May 28, 2012 9:17 am

Opened a bottle of this last night ...

Some initial but very basic thoughts.

Wonderful sweet fruity nose on this. The big sherry fruitiness dominates but more from soft fruits like apricots and peach instead of the usual dried fruit cake mix of traditional oloroso. So I'm guessing the Malaga casks are showing their extra fruity sweetness here. Lovely there is no hiding from it

Again the taste is as big as the nose and in the same vein but with a sweet honey or syrup base . Very silky and coats the mouth but not cloying. However there is a grand big blast of wood spice in there which to me is the ideal off set to balance the sweetness.

The finish starts to dry quickly and even a touch of bitter but some of the sweetness repeats in diminishing waves in and out ... if there is a flaw in this whiskey it may be here. Flaw may be the wrong word but the finish does not live up to the expectation that the nose and taste deliver, maybe it is too delicate to what preceded it. I expected it be long and rewarding but it seems a bit half hearted in comparison to the rest of the whiskey.


Over all this is a wonderful flavourful whiskey and a perfect after dinner whiskey and despite my reservations on the finish is well worth the effort. Maybe my expectations were built up too much and are over riding what I should be getting from the finish and I definitely need to return to it. But be in no way reserved in getting a bottle of this if you can as it is worth the trouble. A wonder experience and already looking forward to having another asap ....
