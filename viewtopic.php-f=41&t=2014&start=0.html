Whiskey Memorabilia

by Good Whiskey Hunting » Tue Aug 13, 2013 11:07 am

I've collected various whiskey memorabilia over the years and thought it would be worth something in time.

I follow various auctions on ebay to see what the fetch. I recently seen a water jug from George Roe and thought it would go well.

I estimated it at over £200 as i'd see rare Jameson ashtrays getting that. I got an auction reminder before the end of the auction and it was at £223.77. I checked to see what the final bid was yesterday and it fetched £847.00

Here's a link http://cgi.ebay.ie/ws/eBayISAPI.dll?Vie ... NA:IE:3160

The Whisky Exchange had a full bottle recently for £899 I think. If I had the cash to spare I'd have bought the bottle.

I'll be looking at more auctions now I think.
