Re: Review of IWS meeting 26th March 2009

by John » Fri Mar 27, 2009 4:40 pm

In a strange sort of way, the old Chinese curse ' may you live in interesting times' might apply here. There is certainly alot of 'interesting' stuff going on at the moment in terms of society protocols, constitutions, bank details, etc, etc and much of this, though absolutely necessary for the formation of any proper institution, could well be regarded as a curse!

Bear with it all, help develop it to its potential and maybe even try to enjoy it! It will all stand us in good stead in the long run

When we get the formalities out of the way, we'll be able to operate in a more time efficient manner, the momentum built up to date will snowball allowing us to get more done and pretty soon we'll be able to devout more and more time within each meeting to learning, nosing ,tasting and appreciating the finer aspects of the whiskeys that are given the honour of being chosen for a society review.

Thanks to all who have put the effort in thus far, it was good to see everyone again and I'm looking forward to the next chapter
