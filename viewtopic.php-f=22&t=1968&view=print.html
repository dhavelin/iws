Good book on whiskey

Posted: Fri Jun 28, 2013 10:40 pm
by mawhinney
Last night at the tasting, I was asked about a good book that would entice a person into exploring and appreciating the joys of whiskey. I could remember the title of such a book but not the bibliographic details. Now here they are.
.
Allhoff, Fritz and Adams, Marcus P (editors) Whiskey and Philosophy. A Small Batch of Spirited Ideas. John Wiley & Sons, Inc. 2010. ISBN 978-0-470-43121-4 (pbk)
"...explores the heart and soul of this heady potation in a lively collection of essays from philosophers, other academics, and whiskey writers." So says the blurb, but it's true!
I am now re-reading Chapter15...The Virtuous Whisky Drinker and Living Well.
