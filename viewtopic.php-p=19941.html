Re: Erin Go Bragh Distilled in 1953 - Valuation?

by TheWhiskeyBro » Sat May 16, 2015 10:42 am

Hi all,

As far as my records show there were three bottlings of "Erin Go Bragh Unblended Pot Still Malt Whiskey" all were bottled at 40% abc and Imported into the U.S. by John Gross & Co. Baltimore Maryland. They were supplied by Stuart McNair & Co. Glasgow. There is also the more common6yo is from Midleton, the others are only attributed to Midleton because of that.

They were also responsible for the "Erin Go Bragh Single Malt Irish Whiskey 6yo" bottled in 1997.

1. 20yo d.1967 b.1987
2. 22yo d.1963 b.1985
3. 22yo d.1953 b.1975

Numbers 2 & 3 are featured in Valentino Zagatti's book.
