Re: Old Bottle of Jameson 15 years

by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Wed Mar 28, 2012 1:38 pm

Fair play for doing the research and it looks right ... as from the bottle style I would of said 50-60's all right.

Can't really see it being earlier.

These are funny bottles. Quite collectable but a value is very difficult to put on them.

As John said above I would say circa €150-200 mark also. However we do see collectors getting carried away at auction and paying much bigger money.

It really depends on interest.

The majority of Irish Whiskey in Ireland was bottled by Independent traders and Publicans right up to the mid 60's.

There is quite a lot of Independently bottled Irish Whiskey out there so I'm not sure why one bottle goes for more than another but I've seen plenty go from the 100-200 mark but then others fly away with some high prices.

Best of luck selling it as it is a nice bottle.
