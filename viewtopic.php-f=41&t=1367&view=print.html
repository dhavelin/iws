The Dungourney can be bought at the distillery (or the distillery's website) for 500 euro, so maybe a little less than that as it's second hand.
Got the Midleton 25 year old at the start of the summer for just under 800.
Got the Midleton 26 year old a couple of months ago for 800 but saw it go at auction for 750.
Was offered the 30 year old for 1,000 but turned it down thinking 900 was a fairer price, but the seller didn't think the same way. So maybe somewhere in between would be accurate for that particular bottle.
