by IrishWhiskeyChaser
The Cooley single casks have not appreciated much and appear not overly collectible but could do better with a little more time. The Bushmills not yet collectable imo.


Spirit Safe Circa €80-120.
Old Midleton Circa €200-300 (It is in Celtic Whiskey Shop for €600 but I'm skeptical as to whether there is demand for it at that price)
Connemara Cask 3713 €80-120 (Possible the first Connemara Singer Cask and was exclusive to Celtic Whiskey Shop)
Willie Napier Circa €500-600 (It is in Celtic Whiskey Shop for €750)
Bushmills 21yo = Although a 2002 bottling, Currently no real collectors value so retail is circa €135
Tyrconnell Cask 3169 €100-150 (This is the first Tyrconnell Single Cask release and was exclusive to Celtic Whiskey Shop)
Connemara Cask Strength €80-120

For insurance purposes I'd take the figures in Bold but for selling on the open market much less in many cases.
