Re: Swap shop / Buy and Sell

by TheWhiskeyBro » Tue Feb 15, 2011 11:15 pm

Of course that rule of thumb really only applies to the retail price.

From around 1996 onwards the maximum you should pay is €200-250.

My own guide for retail would be RRP €145 for 2006-2011 then add €20 for every year prior to that with a slight premium for the 2000 vintage for some reason.

Collectors will generally pick the recent bottlings up for far cheaper than that. The average price I paid for 2000s bottlings is c. €130, the 1990s cost an ave €200. the 1980s bottlings €300 +/- €50.

So as said many times before there is the 'Auction' value, the 'Retail' value and the 'Insurance/Replacement' value if you could get insurance.
