Of course, my wife got the bulk of the cash from the sale. However, the wife doesn't know I pocketed enough money for Whiskey. Well, I DID give up going out to Restaurants, buying Coffee in the morning, etc. I'm having more fun collecting (and drinking) Whiskey/Whisky.
Am I the only one who is this out of control buying/collecting ?

Ah ha! "The Irishman Original Clan" is once again back to it's $27.99 (no tax) price in NH. Sweeeeet!
Perhaps I'll buy a bottle of "The Irishman" and a bottle of Powers! Or...or...I did just open an pour myself a dram of JAMESON 12. Damn fine Whiskey! I realize that I am a newbie regarding tasting Irish Whiskey but, this much I can say...I have now tasted Redbreast, Black Bush, Powers, Jameson 12 and "The Irishman". I believe that each and every single one of the five are OUTSTANDING Irish Whiskeys! I would say that one isn't necessary better than the other, just different. I really think it just depends on ones mood which is prefered.
I enjoy (from time to time) a nice big bowl of quality Ice Cream. I enjoy Chocolate, Vanilla, Strawberry & Coffee. Each unique but equally good (in my book). I have quickly learned that once you reach a certain level of quality in Irish Whiskey, taste becomes rather subjective. Perhaps I would rate (regular) POWERS a bit "lesser" than the other four I've listed but still, quite good, especially considering it's much lower price. I would rate Jameson 12, Black Bush and "The Irishman" damn near equal in quality. These three are (U.S.) priced very close together. Redbreast is a bit more expensive. I killed my first bottle of Redbreast rather quickly. I have put two more away. Considering the lower price of Jameson 12, Black Bush and "The Irishman", it may be awhile before I spent the extra money on Redbreast. Wow, I have to decide which I prefer for a repeat buy tomorrow...Jameson 12,
Black Bush or "The Irishman". Tough call!
