Re: IRISH DISTILLERS - NEW PRICES

Posted: Fri Nov 22, 2013 1:53 pm
by Good Whiskey Hunting
Thanks for bringing this up Luke. I've been ranting to reps about this. The price went up a few month ago. The budget only added €2. I mentioned before about buying Redbreast for €35 on sale in Supervalu a few years back. Now it nearly double the price.

I would normally treat myself and some friends to a few bottles but now I find myself looking for better value. I'm sure lots of others are the same.

You can buy a great Japanese/Scotch of equal age or quality for far less. Luke mentioned a good value Glendronach the other day. The Yamazaki 18 for €115 is an interesting example. The Jameson 18 is €125/130.

I feel Irish whiskey fans are been priced out. This can't be good for the industry. I wonder have the prices affected sales in shops like CWS.

I'm working in the industry too and know we'll suffer because of the increases.

I really fear the are killing the goose that lays the golden egg. They should look at the property bubble alright and think of the bust.
