Re: Irish Whiskey Society first cask bottling

by PureDrop » Sun Aug 30, 2009 6:31 pm

Folks, I've updated the Grand Crew website at http://grandcrew.iwai.ie.
The Gallery includes the first batch of photos from yesterday (no captions yet). See http://grandcrew.iwai.ie/gallery/

Yesterday was a fantastic example of organisations and individuals cooperating enthusiastically to deliver a fantastic result and very very enjoyable day. We also had the usual suspects putting in tremendous effort, Offaly IWAI branch, HBA, the 107B project barge crew and the Kilbeggan Harbour Ameniety Group. The Irish Whiskey Society turned up in numbers to give the casks a good send off. John Teeling of Cooley was very supportive. In addition, we had local individuals like Joe Cleary with his vintage Leyland, Jimmy Murray with his Clydesdale, Carina O'Donovan and her riders volunteering tremendous time and effort. Many thanks to all.

A bottle of Grand Crew (or a promise of same) was presented to the 10th annual AXA/AON Annual Across Ireland run (Irelands largest & Longest motorcycle event of its kind) from Dublin to Galway when it passed through Kilbeggan. The bottle was auctioned for €280 at their charity auction last night.

I'd appreciate if people would send me additional photos for use on the Grand Crew site - send to pics[at]iwai.ie
Its obvious that we're missing some specific photogenic events, e.g. Brian Quinn sealing the casks, casks being rolled off the cart etc.

Michael.
