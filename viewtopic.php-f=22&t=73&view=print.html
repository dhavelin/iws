If anyone is visiting the fine city of Utrecht, you could do worse than to visit Van Dongen NachtegaalStraat Utrecht bus number 3 will take you to this street from the Central Station. They have a good selection of independent bottlings from Signatory etc. While in the Central Station you can visit another shop there which is located at the train station hall. I think it is the traverse section of the Hoog Cathrina Shopping Centre.I cannot remember the name of the shop but it has a great selection of miniatures. There is also a great shop in Amersfoort called Van Weesp. Regards Liam Murrray The Wine Centre Kilkenny

My tip for Amsterdam is

Ton Overmars

Hoofddorpplein 11
1059CV Amsterdam

http://www.tonovermars.nl/index.html

The affable Ton and his wife run this well stocked Whiskey & wine shop.

Ton is always ready to talk whiskey and offer advice.

Get the No 2 tram to Hoofddorpplein 11 - Amsterdam which is a nice little square a couple miles from the city centre.
