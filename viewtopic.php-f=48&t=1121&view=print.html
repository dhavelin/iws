Cadenhead Cooley 13yo World Whiskies 1992 (2005)

Posted: Tue May 03, 2011 4:47 pm
by IrishWhiskeyChaser
Nose: Fruity, Citrus Malt, Apple/pear drops, Nutty, Smokey (like parma ham smokey) & Sulphur, Quite peaty.

Taste: A wonderful mix of above Big and bold.

Finish: A touch spent Matches/Sulphur, Some ash and smoke.

Comment: A more robust version of a Single Cask Connemara. Nice
