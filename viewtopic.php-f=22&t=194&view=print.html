Hi Guys,
Just got given an interesting very rare limited edition expression from my Jameson rep here in Spain.Its Jameson stock that was transported in bulk to be bottled in Spain.But their was an accident and the whiskey that was saved was placed in Navarra Wine Casks and matured for a further 9 years.So apart from the origional vat being matured in Burbon and Sherry,making this a 3 wood.The wine casks were from Palacio de la Vega and the casks were matured in the town of Manzanares.The bottle is sealed and for my collection so i cant give a taste profile but as the bottle is clear it has a georgous dark amber old gold colour.The bottles were given to staff etc and yours truly.
