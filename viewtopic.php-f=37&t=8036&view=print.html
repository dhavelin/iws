I'd love if it continued. Unfortunately there is no one on the committee that uses it. That's been the way for a couple of years now.
It's requires regular maintenance and updates to try and foil hackers and allow other stuff( technical stuff) to happen. I think David, has been called upon to try and keep it going. Without him it would have gone 3 maybe 4 years ago. I remember it being discussed at meetings and fighting for it.
I have been told that it will remain as an archive on the society's website. David is trying to make it as accessible as possible but no further information will be added.

I can't understand why certain things are done by the society sometimes. You know I've been vocal before about it. You're right about the membership card and the gift too. I paid €100's in over the years and get to go to a hard full of tastings because as you rightly said, it's a Dublin society. We're sitting on a tonne of money and yet others are scorned for spending money, they earned by themselves on their members. It's no secret, I love drinking good whiskey, we should splash out a little.

I know most of the current committee and they're determined to address some of the shortcomings brought up at the AGM. By growing it basically fucked itself. Larger tastings need 2 of everything and bigger rooms. We will never be able to recreate some glorious tastings.

I love the society for the friends I've got out of it and the great nights we've shared. The Forum was my way of keeping in touch. Twitter is no place for discussion. Facebook is horrible too. It's impossible to search information like a forum. Both the society's Facebook & Twitter will probably only ever be used for announcements etc.

Fionnan, Jim & many others use "The Pure Pot Still Irish Whiskey Appreciation Club". It's well run and discussions are kept to Irish whiskey alone. We'd love if it was all about Pot Still but nothing is perfect as we've all learned.

Thank you so much for the discussions and help and craic over the years. I'll miss this place.
