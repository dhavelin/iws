Re: Whiskeys of Ireland wooden stand

by IainB » Thu Jul 14, 2011 11:04 am

There's 2 varients out at the moment. One you can buy online from Jameson here:

https://shop.jamesonwhiskey.com/p-32-ja ... linth.aspx

There's also a 6 bottle version of this with Midleton VR, Power 12 and Redbreast 12, though I haven't seen it for sale anywhere.

There were various older wooden versions back from when IDL owned bushmills - I have one with Jameson 12, Redbreast 12, Powers 12, Midleton, Bushmills 10 and Bushmills 16. There was also an older one with 5 bottles. You will sometimes see these in pubs and off licences around the place, for example Next Door in Wicklow as Zoltan mentioned, but I'd imagine it would be hard to get them to part with them at this stage.

If you're not planning on spending a fortune or buying the whiskey that 3 bottle plinth might be a good bet - it actually looks a lot nicer in real life than it does on the photo. Also, if it's whiskey gifts you're after, there's a few other thing on that Jameson site that might be of interest.
