Tullamore's 10 year anniversary

Posted: Fri Nov 12, 2010 4:37 pm
by jcskinner
The Directors of Tullamore Dew Heritage Centre cordially invite you to celebrate the Centre’s 10th anniversary at Tullamore Dew Heritage Centre, Bury Quay, Tullamore on Friday 19th November 2010 at 6.30pm

PROGRAMME
• Welcome from the Chairman
• Demonstration by a cooper
• Talk from local historian Michael Byrne on ‘Distilling in Tullamore and the story of Tullamore Dew’
• Tasting and information session on the Tullamore Dew Irish whiskey range

RSVP: Tullamore Dew Heritage Centre, Bury Quay, Tullamore.
Tel: 057 932 5015 email: manager@tullamore-dew.org
www.tullamore-dew.org www.facebook.com/tullamoredewheritagec
