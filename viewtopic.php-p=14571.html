by JohnM » Sun Aug 19, 2012 7:54 pm

The Master of Malt price is a legacy price. If they were selling it now, they'd definitely be asking for more than that. The Whisky Exchange is one of the only shops selling it right now. This ceramic version, that is. But if it was worth what they're selling it for, they'd have none left.

But as you say, you won't get retail prices for it. There's one on eBay advertised for £150 at the moment. You could see if that sells or what it sells for to give you an indication of value. I doubt it would sell for that, to be honest. I'd say you might get about £100 for it. Selling ceramics can be hard, because it's hard to know how much spirit is left in the container.
