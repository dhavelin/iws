Hi everyone, input required from all quarters,

Later in the Autumn the society are hoping to survey all comers, members, forum, mailing list etc.

The purpose is to help develop the society, continue in the right direction, hear what people want from the society, get constructive feedback and plan for the future

What questions should we be asking?

An online survey of about 20 questions is planned using this tool http://www.surveymonkey.com/ (alternative suggestions welcome)

So please contribute any suggestions for questions, or feedback that the society requires!
