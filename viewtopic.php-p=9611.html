RiRa Irish Pub and Whiskey Bar to Open Mar 2011 in Las Vegas

by mulltea » Thu Feb 10, 2011 12:55 am

The opening of RiRa Irish Pub and Whiskey Bar in Las Vegas, Nevada is set for March 2011. It will open in Mandalay Place right next door to the Guinness Retail Store where you can find fully licensed Guinness merchandise. Please visit this great city and visit RiRa's whiskey bar to share your whiskey knowledge and experiences. It aims to be a whiskey society mecca where information is shared and whiskey is enjoyed to the fullest potential.

Finally back online, fellow whiskey friends. When the opening occurs next Tuesday we will feature a 1951 Knappogue Castle very rare Whiskey. Distilled in 1951 and bottled in 1987. We hope to achieve a list of 150 before the end of March. Among that list will be several Knappogue Castle varieties and a Tyrconnell line. I am very excited and learning new material everyday. Thank you very much for the well wishes and I hope to have more interesting information posted soon. Talk soon.
