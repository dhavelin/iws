JAMESON 12 and BLACK BUSH and...

by SixCats! » Sun Jul 04, 2010 2:27 pm

Hi all,

Long story short. I have both Jameson 12 and Black Bush. Being new to Whiskey drinking/tasting I have discovered that some evenings, I prefer Black Bush while other nights, I prefer Jameson 12. IMHO, one dram is not better than the other, just...different and both excellent! Here in Northern New England both Jameson 12 and Black Bush can be purchased (on sale in NH) $30.99 a 750ml bottle. These two drams have become my (go to) Irish Whiskey foundation. However, money being tight (and since my drams have grown from one ounce to three) I now also buy POWER Gold Label which is much less expensive. I enjoy the POWER but, when I can afford to buy the Jameson 12 or Black Bush, I go the extra mile. Oooh, I also still enjoy "The Irishman" Irish Whiskey as well and look forward to the next sale. I have been thinking I should try the standard Kilbeggan which is the least expensive Irish Whiskey available in Maine. Kilbeggan has had some good reviews/press here in the U.S. but, I fear the reviews might by nothing but marketing hype. Oooh well, I guess there is only one way to find out.
It's a dangerous job but, somebody's got to do it!

Regards,
