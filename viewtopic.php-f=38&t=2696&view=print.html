If your in Kenmare and Dingle make sure you pass through Killarney and visit the new Irish Whiskey Experience on New St, just off the Main St, Ireland's largest selection of Irish Whiskey, they do food, tastings and they have a sherry barrel on the premises where you can fill your own, definitely worth checking out.
