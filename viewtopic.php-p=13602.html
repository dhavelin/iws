Re: Whiskey Live 2012

by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Thu Apr 26, 2012 9:24 am

All Master Classes now finalised.

Just wondering if your going to the Day even can you go to an evening master class???

ALL VIP TICKET HOLDERS MUST ARRIVE AT 2PM FOR DAY SESSION OR 6PM FOR EVENING SESSION AT BUSWELLS HOTEL http://www.buswells.ie/ for their VIP Masterclass. After the class you will then be escorted to The Mansion House for Whisky Live Dublin.


TDay MC2 - Masterclass from Cooley Distillery

4.30 - 5.15pm in Room 2

From the award winning Cooley Distillery, Global Brand Ambassador John Cashman will take you through a tasting of four unique world class Irish whiskeys. The perfect introduction to the story of Irish whiskey that includes the history of the category right up to the present day and the road ahead. Join John as he introduces, the four most renowned Irish whiskeys from Cooley, Greenore Single Grain, Kilbeggan Irish whiskey, Tyrconnell Single Malt and Connemara Peated Single Malt. All €15

Day MC3 - The 'Spot' whiskey range - an in-depth tasting

4.45 - 5.30pm in Room 1

Trace the origins and development of the Green Spot whiskey range with a focus on the whiskey components that combine to create this wonderful old whiskey - delivered by Kevin O'Gorman, Warehousing & Maturation Manager, Midleton Distillery.

Evening MC1 - Masterclass from Beam Global

7.30 - 8.15pm in Room 2

A special evening treat, meet John Cashman, Global Brand Ambassador for Irish whiskey within Beam Global as he takes on Simon Brooking, Scotch Master Ambassador and Fred Booker Noe, the great grandson of Jim Beam and the Jim Beam Small Batch Bourbon Distiller for Beam Global. A lively whiskey debate is sure to appeal as each country fights its corner on whose whiskey reigns above all – with you, the guests, as the Judges!

Evening MC3 - The Jameson Reserves Tasting

8.45 - 9.30pm in Room 2

A journey through the Jameson Reserves range from classic Jameson to Jameson 18 Year Old, with a special focus on the new release: Jameson 'Select Reserve' - delivered by David Quinn, Master of Whiskey Science Midleton Distillery.
