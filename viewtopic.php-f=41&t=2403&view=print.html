I'd expect they are worth €300-400 for a private sale. They retail much higher but shops have overheads and VAT.
I see some go at auction a while back for €350 including commission etc. The condition was fair too.

If yours was one that had a Crystal cut glass included in the package those are rarer and worth a little more. All would depend on bottle condition and packaging.
