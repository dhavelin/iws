by JohnM
That's a real gem. They have only just revived the brand, but it will probably taste little like your original one. It was made at the Jameson Distillery in Bow Street for Mitchell and Sons. Or, they used to buy whiskey from the Jameson and put it in their casks and bottle it themselves. You might come across a bottle like this again, but it would be pretty rare and you'd have to pay a pretty penny for it.

The new version is available in Ireland anyway. You can get it at Mitchell and Sons or the Celtic Whiskey Shop in Dublin. It's made at the Midleton Distillery. The websites say it's out of stock, but I think there's a few bottles available. It will be made in batches, so maybe there will be a new batch out soon.
