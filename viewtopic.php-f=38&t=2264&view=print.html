Whisky L! Shanghai

Posted: Wed Jul 16, 2014 10:17 am
by Snaffled
The Whisky Live website says the events in China in 2014 are postponed to 2015, and yet there is a Whisky L! event in the same venues in Shanghai & Beijing. Strange.

Anyway, my point is that that it is a shame that again this year it looks like zero representation from any Irish Whiskey at the event. There is a wide range of Scotch from the different areas, a significant number of US bourbons and some Japanese single malts. Pernod Ricard, Diageo and Suntory Beam are all well represented, so I had hoped they might stick a few bottles of Irish into their suitcase for the event as well, but it looks like a potentially huge market will remain still largely unaware of Irish Whiskey.

Many bars have the obligatory Jameson, and while it is a good Whiskey, there is such an increasing range of styles available, its a pity none of them are getting represented.
