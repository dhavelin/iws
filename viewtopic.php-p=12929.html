Re: Ladies and Whiskey

by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Wed Dec 21, 2011 10:37 am

Hi Ulli & great to have you on board, Céad Míle Fáilte (One Hundred Thousand Welcomes as the blarney goes)

The quick answer is No you won't be the only Lady in the group but you will be one of a few. We hope to change this and are actively trying to encourage this. We have quite a few signed up to the forum though but still need a few more fully signed up for the Society to break the stereo type. Many still think whiskey is a man's drink over a certain age ... whiskey is for every one but sometimes that is hard to convey to an uneducated public. We have a wide demographic from college going to retired and all in-between and every one gets along in a friendly atmosphere but I suppose the biggest group would be the 35-45 age group. Hopefully you will encourage others to join also just by posting here. One of the girls from last year is already on the committee and if your interested I'm sure there is more room for you to come on board too as we are always looking to improve.

I'm based in Galway so only get the chance to come to a tasting once in the blue moon but they are still for me the best way to learn about new whiskey at a very reasonable price so for me €50 is worth the membership even for just 2 tastings.

If you are looking for an ice breaker we can arrange for one of the committee members to meet you at your first tasting and let you know the format and introduce you to a few of the members and get you settled in before the event.
