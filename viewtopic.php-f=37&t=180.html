Indeed, this is a topic that has come up at the committee meetings. We want the website to be the ultimate reference for Irish whiskey so history, brands, bottlings, etc. should all appear eventually. There will also be a record of the society's tastings and more info on upcoming events.

Various people have promised to contribute the content above. In the meantime, I'm spending as much time as I can to add support for these things to the site.

Also, while the look of the website is pretty spartan at the moment, that will change when we get a design identity for the society (logo, colour scheme, font, etc.).

Stay tuned!
