Whiskey Tasting Wicklow 24 October

Posted: Sun Oct 12, 2014 7:14 pm
by TWR
A Curious Case of Whiskeys in the Night!
A Whiskey Tasting Experience will take place on Friday 24th October 2014 at 8.30 pm in The Coffin Shed (Behind the Cartoon Inn), Rathdrum, Co. Wicklow.

John Cashman, Global Brand Ambassador for Beam Suntory Whiskeys will deliver an exciting programme introducing participants to the finer points of the various types of Whiskeys available. Those attending will be treated to a fine selection of whiskeys, including a whiskey cocktail on arrival. There will also be a prize draw raffle on the night with some very attractive prizes. It promises to be an entertaining and informative night for anyone who enjoys the occasional glass of the “Water of Life”!

Tickets cost €25 and are available from The Cartoon Inn, or by emailing whiskey24oct@gmail.com. All proceeds will go towards the St Saviours National School Building Fund, which is established to assist the school build a PE hall as part of the current classroom extension project.
All times are UTC [ DST ]
