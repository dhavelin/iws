by jcskinner » Fri Nov 06, 2009 8:58 pm

"What your Da drank" - Sixties, Seventies and earlier bottlings, possibly compared alongside their modern namesakes?

That could be a good, relatively inexpensive tasting, especially if we look to go with Sixties versions of Powers, Bushmills, Tullamore Dew, etc. Even an early Jameson wouldn't raise the cost prohibitively. 'Redbreast, Comber or other rarer whiskeys could quickly make it a much less affordable evening, though. I suspect I could near enough supply all the older bottles myself, and likely there are a few other members who could contribute too.

I'd be thinking along the lines of White Bush, Bush liqueur (forerunner to Black Bush), Jameson 12 or 15, Powers, Tullamore Dew versus their modern bottlings.
