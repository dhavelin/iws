Re: Whiskey for Whisky Bible 2013...

by Dram Good Research » Wed Feb 29, 2012 10:15 am

Thanks folks, I'll look into all those...

Leo, we're concentrating on whiskeys that are currently available in the worldwide market (country specific exclusives still count!) - Irish retastes were few and far between last year as Jim was saying, so we're aiming to be as comprehensive as possible in finding whiskeys to be revisited, new expressions released over the past year or two and ones that have simply gone under our radar.

Core brands can be sent through to us without hesitation, limited editions/single casks should be no more than a couple of years old so we have the benefit of relevance to our readers.

The cut off date for samples this year is not set in stone, but will be mid-summer around June/July at this point. We were a little late in going to print last year due to various delays...

Pop me a message and I'll let you know our current office address and contact details if you need them. Cheers!
