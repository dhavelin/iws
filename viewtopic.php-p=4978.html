Re: Midleton Distillery - Newstalk

by DavidH » Sat Apr 24, 2010 12:47 pm

Some notes...

Midleton Visitor centre opened in 1992, now gets about 100,000 visitors per year.

Morehead said Midleton is the "Home of Pure Pot Still". [We know that's the case but it's great to hear them push that in public.]

IDL sold 0.5m cases of whiskey when Pernod-Ricard took in 1988. Sold over 2.7m last year and hope for 3m this year. Hope for 1m cases in the US alone this year.

90% of production is for export, 10% domestic.

Powers, Paddy... "still extremely important". Powers is selling well in the US, Paddy in France... all have a big part to play in the future.

Jameson is about 5 years old.

Going back to 7 day production in the coming weeks.
