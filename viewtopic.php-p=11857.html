Re: Connemara Bog Oak

by John » Mon Nov 07, 2011 9:40 pm

Truthfully I'm also incredibly dubious about what bog oak, an organic material that has been subject to natural decomposition and subsequent acidic leeching under water, could offer to a whiskey. I like the idea, but I can't help but feel that the spirit would have benefited much more from a more imaginative, lively, wood maturation. The price tag of €250 to me signals that this is a once off, an experiment that really isn't intended to be repeated. Or maybe it's just the going rate for a whiskey that's sold with a stave section!!
