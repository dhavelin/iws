Looking forward to this and as regards price:

IDL are going to keep pushing up price for two reasons:

1. Higher price = Whiskey appear more luxury / limited / a bottle to really treat yourself etc

2. Main Reason: Asian Market - they cant get enough irish whiskey! Therefore demand for more exports is out stripping supply as with delays in putting some whiskies into certain markets!

IDL are expanding warehouses regularly also and it all leads to one point demand is ahead of supply. To keep the market on a consistent boom your regular jameson etc needs to remain competitive.

However the more exclusive whiskeys can be priced upwards as they will sell as the market for luxury irish whiskey is on an alarming rise in my opinion worldwide.

Yes we may not be happy with rises but IDL can afford to charge them unfortunatly!
