Being aware of the value and being able to realise the value are 2 very different things.

Further if you see a price in a retail shop that is not an actual value but a retail price. Private Collectors are not willing to pay such prices as they are usually seen as inflated.

Therefore you may need to manage you expectations. I see a 1992 is 429.99 & a 1994 is 419.99 in the celtic whiskey shop. To go through one of these shops you will be offer significantly less possibly less than half as vat will take a big chunk of the above price.

You will be lucky to get anywhere near that in my opinion and if approached here I would think 300-350 should be your bench mark.
