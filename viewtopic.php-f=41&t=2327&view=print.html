Hi Joe,

These days your best bet is using forums like ours and you can do so here as long as prices and negotiations are not discussed openly on this forum.
It's alright for look for ballpark values of bottles as this is quite subjective but not ok to make or recieve any direct offers.

However you can also contact Specialist retailers like The Celtic Whiskey Shop on Dawson st in Dublin or The Whisky Exchange in London to see if they would be interested.

There are also some specialist auctions for whiskey too but your main issue would be getting your bottle to the aution.

McTears in Scotland
Whiskyauction.com (Germany)
Whiskeyauction.co.uk (UK)

Feel free to post a list
