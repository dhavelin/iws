Re: IDL Single Pot Still website gone live!

by Raven » Mon Sep 24, 2012 9:12 pm

History section has been updated '1617' with reference to some detail that has turned up from
research in Youghal where Richard Boyle 1st.Earl of Cork had a distillery in the early 1600's.
Boyle has recorded in his personal diary what is perhaps the first ever recorded export of
Whiskey to the Americas. Sent as provisions for Raleigh's ship on his last voyage of discovery to the Guineas
(his friend Sir Walter Raleigh (Lord Mayor of Youghal 1588/89 and from whom Boyle had bought his estate).
I do not believe that the SWA will be able to get an earlier reference for an export of this type to the
America's !!! So perhaps poor old Walt's failure to find 'El Dorado' etc. may be attributed to his being on the
'batter' with 'Choice aquavitae' from Boyle's Youghal Distillery
