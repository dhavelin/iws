Hi and welcome.

You could check the Celtic Whiskey Shop. The Jameson's go for various prices. I know they had a Jameson like the one you discribed for €300 a while back. That's retail price so they may offer half that or more.

If you try the auction site you'll have to pay commission and there's a risk they won't get the price you hope for.

The Midleton's are in the same boat. I recommend looking at whiskyauction.com and check their results for both bottles. They have a limited edition Jameson on the current auction.

I considered using them a few years back but the costs of sending and commission meant it wasn't worth while.

Best wishes
