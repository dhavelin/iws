Shock Horror - Scotch Irish whiskey

by shanel23 » Thu Jul 08, 2010 10:11 am

OK - am down in Madrid on business and went for a nice meal with a group from Germany , UK and Ireland - so had a nice meal and we were going to have an after dinner drink ( I voted whiskey but we ended up having a brandy - Carlos 1 ( excellent by the way ))

Then the horror moment when one of the Irish guys says we should also have a scotch - fine I said - then he suggested a Powers or a Paddy ( both were on the shelf ) . I informed him in no uncertain terms that they were both Irish whiskies and he said he knew they were Irish but where he is from ( Roscommon ) these are known as Scotch whiskies as they are made "like a scotch" ( his words )

Very surprised that this type of thinking is out there
