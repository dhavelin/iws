For the dreamers amongst you, There's a nice little glossy book out called The Art of Distilling Whiskey and other spirits
It will keep the dreamers among you happy for a while. Its mainly an up to date expose on artisan distillation in the USofA.

All you have to do is buy someplace like this, a little still, a license, a big sweeping brush, a few bags of malt, a few old casks and bobs your uncle.

It (the book) is a nicer production than the Alaskan Bootlegger's Bible

Once you're over that and have the Lagavullin DE poured, you can then tuck into ...Whiskey and Philosophy: A Small Batch of Spirited Ideas and fall asleep with a smile on your face.
