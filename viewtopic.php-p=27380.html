THIS FORUM IS CLOSING!

by DavidH » Fri Mar 02, 2018 11:21 pm

Dear Irish Whiskey fan,

This forum will be closed to all new discussion from next weekend (10 March 2018).

The world moves on and the society running its own server, database and forum software is an anachronism. There are other venues for discussion on the internet these days and most users have already migrated there. The Irish Whiskey Society itself has outposts on Facebook and Twitter.

The plan is to preserve the information and chat in this forum by taking a snapshot of it before it closes. We will then upload the snapshot to the same web address (that's our hope anyway).

Thank you very much for all your contributions over many years.

The forum is dead, long live Irish whiskey discussion!
Website: Liquid Irish
Twitter: @LiquidIrish
