Apologies to anyone who saw an earlier version of this post. The Vom Fass shop on South Anne Street in Dublin hasn't yet got a spirit licence - it is selling wines and liqueurs up to 20% ABV in strength - but its sister shop in the UK is offering 4 and 8 year old Irish single malts.

Vom Fass is a "bottle shop", similar to ones I've seen in Germany. They have vats of different liquids - alcoholic drinks, olive oils, vinegars - and they will fill a bottle. The bottle costs extra but you could bring it back for a refill.

I'm not sure whether the malt is from Cooley or Bush.
