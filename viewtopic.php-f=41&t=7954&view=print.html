Welcome on board.

There's a good selection of single casks available. Redbreast have 3 out, the Celtic Whiskey Shop, The Whisky Exchange & La Maison du Whisky. The prices are high at €300/200/500. Oddly the €500 was the first to sell. Power's have a few out too. It seems pubs are releasing them. It adds to the number of releases but can be a little frustrating. Midleton have been doing it for years and they fetch good money at auctions.
It's an expensive time to start collecting Irish Whiskey.

Some if the newer distilleries will be releasing limited first editions but they too will be at premium prices. Only time will tell if they're a worthy investment.

Best of luck with it. Don't jump in without research is my best recommendation.
