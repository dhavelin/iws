Re: Midleton Very Rare 2011

Posted: Wed Dec 14, 2011 11:49 pm
by kurtbenoit
There may be other people on this site who can offer more advice than me in terms of sales methods. You've placed it in the collector's corner which is a good start. If you are in Dublin you could call to the Celtic Whiskey Shop on Dawson Street, the proprietor, Ally, might be willing to purchase it of you. Unfortunately even though it was the very first Midleton there are usually one or two of them floating about for sale and easy to find. The 85' and 86 are much harder to find. And can usually command a higher sale price. Also some of the more popular whisky websites, masters of malt and whisky exchange have a rare bottle section where you can email them and they might make you an offer for what you have to sell.
