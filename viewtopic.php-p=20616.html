New Whiskey & New Committee

The Society's latest Bottling was unveiled. It's a Midleton 18 year old Grain, yes Midleton Grain. This is the first ever release of a Grain whiskey from them.

That was the first of the night. It wasn't numbered. I tried it last as I'd missed the beginning.

Here's the order.
1-Tullamore Black 43
2-Irishman 12 limited edition.
3-Green Spot 10
4-Dair Ghaelach Tree 4 C/S

I'd only tried the Irishman, Green Spot & Redbreast before. I was impressed by all. The Midleton Dair Ghaelach was voted favourite. I believe the Glendronach was well in the running too. Good idea bringing in the Scottish Whisky to mix things up.
