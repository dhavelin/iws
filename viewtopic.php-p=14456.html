by DavidH » Wed Aug 08, 2012 12:00 pm

I've added details and pics of last month's tasting to the event page:

http://www.irishwhiskeysociety.com/even ... 2012-07-26

These things take longer as I get older. In my defence, there were 11 whiskeys on parade!

What a great evening. The kind of tasting that would make you excited by whiskey all over again. For those who weren't there, the highlights (for me and according to the voting too) were the distilled IPA and stout from Charbay Distillery, plus the live Skype link-up with Charbay's distiller, Marko.

Fionnán collected the whiskeys and the thorough behind-the-scenes production details. Cracking tasting notes too. He does an awesome presentation
