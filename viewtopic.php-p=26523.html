I found Tree 1 last summer in Chicago. Then Tree 6 on the east coast...plenty of availability.

When I called Midleton gift shop, they told me Tree 8 went to South Africa...not south America as I had assumed. Then i scowered the net and anything in south africa. Nothing. So I tried South America (why not). Still no luck! I was completely confused at this point.

Sorry, for the long answer but I am still on the hunt. Should i turn up on another bottle ill post it here.
