Yesterday was a fantastic example of organisations and individuals cooperating enthusiastically to deliver a fantastic result and very very enjoyable day. We also had the usual suspects putting in tremendous effort, Offaly IWAI branch, HBA, the 107B project barge crew and the Kilbeggan Harbour Ameniety Group.
