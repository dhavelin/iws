Re: Bowes or The Fitzwilliam Hotel?

by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Sat Apr 18, 2009 9:28 pm

Food and price aside I've gone for the Fitzwilliam.


As a venue I feel the The Fitzwilliam rooms are far better for tasting than the funtion room in Bowes.

Main reason is the rooms in the Fitzwilliam are more odour neutral. They are also more relaxed and nice surrounds in my opinion of course

Also remember that we are expected to buy a couple of drinks in Bowes so the cheap room is a false economy. However that probably would not apply for Ally's tastings.
