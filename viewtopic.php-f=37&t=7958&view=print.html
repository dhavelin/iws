Proposed change to society structure - vote by 15 September

Posted: Fri Sep 01, 2017 12:30 am
by rathbeggan
It is a pity the current committee isn't sufficiently interested in or engaged with the society membership to provide information about the vote to change the structure of the Irish Whiskey Society. A meeting was held three months ago prior to a Dublin tasting but the meeting was not initiated by the committee, it was held in response to concerns raised by some members, and the opportunity to vote on the change had to be dragged out of them. There have been brief verbal updates at tastings in Dublin, and scant mentions in society e-mails, but in general getting information about this has been like pulling teeth. The link below mentions "a document sent to you in an e-mail" which I certainly have not received yet (as of 00.25 on 1 September 2017) {EDIT - e-mail now issued - see new post below]. This vote was also not mentioned at the Dublin tasting on 31 August 2017.

Please note you have to log into the website (as a member of the IWS) rather than to the forum to be aware of this vote. Only IWS members (rather than forum members) have a vote, which is appropriate.

https://www.irishwhiskeysociety.com/event-2631411
