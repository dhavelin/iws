Tullamore's new whiskey bar

Posted: Tue Apr 29, 2014 12:40 pm
by Brewery Tap
This is my first post to your society forum. In the past few months I have been putting together a top quality selection of the best of Irish Whiskey's. I have been a member of the Irish Whiskey Trail for the past 4 years and I have been advised by Heidi Donelon on what she would perceive has the best 'Wish List' to compile for my pub. With our new Tullamore Dew distillery well on its way we are expecting a large number of whiskey enthusiasts to come our way and it's very important that we are ready to be able to provide them with the best quality Whiskey's and information to enhance their visit to Tullamore. It's a very exciting time for Irish Whiskey and a we are so proud that Grants have decided to bring distilling back to Tullamore. If members of the society would like to make a planned visit here to Tullamore I would be only too happy to entertain you here in the Tap and you can sample the best of my range at very good value for members. (I may also be able to organise a trip to the distillery also) I would really appreciate it if members would let me know if they are interested in this. I have posted a picture of my range on my Brewery Tap Facebook page.
Regards Paul Bell.
