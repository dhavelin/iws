Preview: Irish Whiskey Guide

by TheWhiskeyBro » Sun Jun 14, 2009 12:04 pm

Peter Mulryan's Irish Whiskey Guide is now available in the CelticWhiskeyShop and Hodges & Figges amongst other outlets at approx €6.99.

Pocket sized introduction to Irish Whiskey

Title: Irish Whiskey Guide
Author: Peter Mulryan
Publisher: Appletree Press Ltd
Pages: 96
Cover: Softback
ISBN: 978-1-84758-120-4
Price: €6.95 - €7.50
Published: 2009
Official Release Date: 30 June 2009

This small reference book contains six chapters,

(1) The History of Irish Whiskey
(2) The Types of Whiskey
(3) How Whiskey is Made
(4) The Influences on Malt Whiskey
(5) The Enjoyment of Irish Whiskey
(6) Visiting Distilleries

Review: This is an excellent introduction to Irish Whiskey. It covers all the basic facets of Irish Whiskey in a clear, easy to read, concise style. It is a very good roadmap for the Irish Whiskey novice which as the author himself puts it "If it sparks your imagination there's a lot more to learn, if not then I heartily recommend it as a coaster."

So if you don't know your malt from your pure pot still or your grain from your blends, this is a great little book to commence your journey into the evolving world of what Irish Whiskey means today.
