What's the difference?

Is there one?

This came up in another topic earlier so I thought I put it out there. I've no well formed view yet.

I'm not saying we should quantify it or anything but in my mind there's a difference between a bar that happens to have a few decent whiskeys,, which are often obvious choices - e.g. plinths etc., and one which make a genuine effort to have a decent selection with a few more unusual bottles. They don't have to be of the standard of Tigh Niechtain in Galway or anything, just a few of interest.

Any thoughts?
