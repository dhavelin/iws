That a Late 70's bottle I think.

The screw cap arrived in the late 60's I think. The bottles had the 1784 instead of 1608 until the mid 70's when they found another 176 years of history. There was no one to object to them calling themselves the oldest distillery since Lockes closed in the late 50's.

It has 70 Proof which would have changed in 1980 I think. I stand to be corrected.

I think it may be an exported bottle, Irish bottles were 70cl then I think. Where did your Father in Law get the bottle?

I've never seen a label like that with the 3 stars on top. I've seen later ones have 'The Original' and some earlier ones have 9 year old.
