my mom and dad were here for an xmas visit. me and my dad were able to taste a good amount from my own collection and freeney's in galway. we also did the jameson tastings in dublin. here is a list of all that were tasted. obviously, many were already tasted many a times. twas nice to taste the jameson 12yo distillery reserve. as far as i know, its only sold at the distillery in dublin. was a big fan of green spot and lockes. wish i would have gotten to taste the locke's 8yo single malt.

jameson, jameson crested ten, jameson 12yo special reserve, jameson 12yo distillery reserve, jameson gold reserve, jameson 18yo limited reserve, midleton very rare, paddys, powers, powers 12yo, white bush, black bush, bushmills 10yo malt, green spot, redbreast 12yo, tyrconnell 10yo single malt sherry cask, tullamore dew, locke's, connemara peated single malt
