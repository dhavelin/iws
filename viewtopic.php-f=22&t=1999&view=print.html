It's bourbon-matured Cooley malt. I think most people agree it's pretty good around this age, and that it's possible to select some particularly fine single casks.

I would recommend anyone to try The Palace whiskey, particularly since it's hard to get aged, unpeated, unfinished Cooley malt generally. Tyrconnell is young and their ten year-olds are finished in various woods.
