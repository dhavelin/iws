Hi all,
I mentioned in my introduction that I am of Irish descent but now residing in Norway. I have two friends here that have decided to indulge themselves by purchasing a barrel of whiskey that they then wish to have bottled and labelled for distribution to friends and family. There are several Scottish distilleries that offer this service but I am unable to find one in Ireland. If a barrel can indeed be purchased, they would like to travel to Ireland, visit the distillery and see the sights for a week or so. I’ve been offered paid passage to accompany them as a guide – and am therefore very keen to make this happen…
Is this something the society can assist me with?

First update...
I've had a good look through the website and familiarised myself with the Founding Fathers concept.
I've 'signed up' on the Dingle Founding Fathers website and perused the prospectus.
My friends thought that Dingle's small size and location at the very western edge of Europe added an 'exotic' appeal and we are going ahead as planned.
The concept is one owner / one cask, but I emailed the distillery and there is no problem for a group of friends to share ownership.
That pretty much settled it for us - we're off salmon fishing next week and will have to agree on a name for our cask...
