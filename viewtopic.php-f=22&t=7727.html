Not sure it is worth a whole pile to be honest but it is an interesting bottle all the same. Where did was it bought/brought from? And where are you based?

However as regards to old bottlings it would not be considered very old. Maybe from the 90's or even 80's but not a great age for such a common type of product.

This may have some curiosity value though because of the unusual bottle size.
