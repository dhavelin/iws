Last stuff out of Coleraine was in 1964 just before the Charrington takeover.
Highly unlikely it is a Coleraine product as that bottling only contains whiskey no
older than 6 years old ( blend of malt & grain ). This blend was launched just
to keep the old name alive. Bottling for both Bushmills & Coleraine used to be
done in Coleraine --- not sure if this is still the case !
