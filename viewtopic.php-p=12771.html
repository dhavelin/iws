Re: 1973 MIDLETON PURE POT STILL IRISH WHISKEY

by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Fri Feb 17, 2012 2:10 pm

That first price is a retail price and you will not realise that on the open market.

Original selling price on this was £300 IR pounds which equated to €381 Euro when we changed over to the Euro. And yes it did retail at €381 Euro at that stage

Over the years it has been subject to Irish Distillers Ltd price increases which has more to do with elevating the it to a premium level and that is fair enough.

However as this was available at a much lower cost up to 4 or 5 years ago iris Whiskey collectors will not offer anywhere near the current retail price. It seems to be averaging out at €800 on the open market. However another issue you have is that you are in Oz and this could well be another barrier to you selling it at even that open market price I suggested.

Best of luck selling though.
