As you have probably noticed IDL have turned their attention towards Powers. In the last few years Redbreast and Powers have had a few extra additions.

Powers has always been considered a more traditional style and less civilised than Jameson and therefore not as easy to sell to the uninitiated.
Lots of enthusiasts have been calling for a non chilled filter Standard Whiskey. We've got Powers NAS blend being released as a NCF blend.
We also wanted a reasonably priced NAS Pot Still at an affordable price but their Powers Signature Reserve hasn't filled this gap. They're now releasing a new addition to the range, the Three Swallows. This should be very close to an older style of Powers.

I believe this was what I wished for when I blew out the candles on my birthday cake. Next up a date with Megan Fox and a bottle of Midleton Peril.
