Bushmills 1989 Single Cask (7437) Sherry Hogshead 53.7% Bot 2005

Nose: Spirity numbed by the flat malt. Toffee with Sherry trying hard to break through and a touch of Vanilla

Taste: A really rich buttery mouth feel. Continued from the nose the sherry is to the fore this time but not over powering and intertwines (sic) nicely with the toffee.

FInish And a lovely tongue tingle pleasent and not harsh. Quite dry but the sweetness comes back also and both linger. A bit of dry ash or sulphur feeling also.

Comment: Wonderful ... This is how Bushmills should be
