On a side note, i always buy from the UK as its much cheaper but was in Cork today (as the missus was doing the mini marathon) and dropped into a certain off-license on Oliver Plunkett street and they were charging a crazy €64 for a standard 10 YO Laphroiag and €64 for 10 YO Talisker

I was in Killarney a few weeks back, bringing the young one to see Jedward, thought I would drop into Tesco on the way home - lucky I did - they had 2 bottles of 10 YO Laphroaig sitting on the shelf 'reduced to clear' - €15 each
I swooped them up and asked the guy working in the booze section if there was anymore - he told me that they only ever had 2 and they have been sitting there for the last 3-4 years or so. Couldnt believe it. Even the bottle caps were a different colour and the little leaflet inside was different.
I contacted Laphroaig direct as there was no pin number to earn my points and when I explained the bottle she laughed and said she hasnt seen one of them for a long time but she gave me the points anyway.
Seriously couldnt believe it - €15 on a lucky days shopping versus todays shock at €64 in Cork city.

By the way, there is a bottle of Connemara in Super Value that I have being eyeing up for the last 2 or 3 weeks - €31 - not sure to go for it as I am fairly broke from my last order - my gut is telling me to wait for the Turf Mor.
