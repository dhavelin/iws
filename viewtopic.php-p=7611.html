Nutshell History

DWD is the abbreviation of Dublin Whiskey Distillery Company Ltd. And was located in the Jones Rd Distillery in Dublin. Established 1873 and closed in the 1940's ... possibly 1945. It was considered one of the Dublin big 4 .... the power house distilleries of the Irish DIstilling industry. In this grouping you also have Powers, John Jameson & lesser know William Jameson (Closed 1920's) . It amalgamated with WIlliam Jameson and Geo Roe (another Dublin Distillery) to form DDC Dublin Distillers Company ltd. However all continued to bottle under their own names which is probably why they did not survive. The Jones Road Distillery was the last to close with the othe 2 closing in the 1920's.



It would be very interesting to see a picture of this bottle and we may be able to date it for you.
