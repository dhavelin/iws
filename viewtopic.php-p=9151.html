Hi Pat,

That could be a find all right, I have researched Knappogue's and in particular the private bottlings but have never come across your bottle before.

Apart from the 'usual' 1951, i have traced a 1949, two 1950s and a 1958. Yours would be the first 1964 I have come across.

Mr Andrews who owned Knappogue Castle in the 1970s was very interested in whiskey and purchased many casks for his private use.

We will probably not be able to verify the bottle completely but if you can post a picture i'll do my best to help.
