Here's another Whiskey shop in Germany I found. I know it in Bruehl not Berlin but trying to revive so old treads.
The web shop is still under construction but they have a few odd ones in there.
Free delivery for purchases over €80 too, I wonder does that apply for here.

The most interesting thing I found was that they don't list Tullamore Due at all.

Here's the ones I'd never heard of
A Drop of the Irish 11 years old - Peated - Cask No. 1434 - Limited to 278 bottles. The Bottler is Blackadder Irland.46,0 % 0,70 l 51,50 €
and this cask strength one
A Drop of the Irish 14 years old- Cask No. 253 - Limitiert auf 261 Flaschen.58,2 % 0,70 l 71,90 €

Their from Bushmills and here's a brief description from the site"At Blackadder International, we want you to celebrate that difference but we also want you to enjoy Irish whiskey as it was always traditionally enjoyed."
