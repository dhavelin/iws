Re: Redbreast 12 Price Increase

by Pudge72 » Tue Oct 02, 2012 8:54 pm

Sometime in the past month or so (I believe) the LCBO (the government operated liquor monopoly in Ontario) kicked up the price on this bottle from the $49 first mentioned in this thread to now $55. Basically a 20% mark-up in about nine months (it was $46 last Christmas). Hopefully, this is the end of the increases for the foreseeable future. To be fair to IDL, the pricing changes are often at the whim of the LCBO as they set the shelf price, and the wholesaler/distributor picks up the extra profit...it's a bass ackward situation here.

The Jameson 12 comes in at $50, so the difference in price is now similar to what IWC mentioned earlier in this thread. I don't know how (if at all) the J12 has changed over the same time period.
