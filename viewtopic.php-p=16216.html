I agree with JohnM, we would be thrilled to have gotten single casks a couple of years ago. So these casks are hugely welcome in that context.

Pricing of course is another issue altogether, and it is disappointing to have the single casks out of reach of most. The society members are not the target audience for these bottlings but rather those who make a once off super premium purchase annually imho.

I for one am eternally grateful for the society cask, and it gave all members a chance to sample a superlative whiskey without having to pay the prices discussed here.

I can only compare the Midleton Single Casks to the Diageo Special Releases in terms of price, they sell out quickly but many fans bemoan the pricing.
