IWS Whiskey of the Year 2011 (Results)

Posted: Fri Dec 02, 2011 11:04 am
by IrishWhiskeyChaser
Okay the results are in and we have a clear winner ... a solid second place and joint 3rd places.

The Irish Whiskey Society's Irish Whiskey of the year is ...

1st Place

Power's 12yo John's Lane Release 46%

2nd Place

Redbreast 12 Cask Strength

3rd Place

Connemara Bog Oak
Tyrconnell 11yo Sherry Finish Whisky Live Ed


The voting was put to members and 17 responded.

Members were to vote for the 3 favourite releases of the year. A very simple system of members asked to vote 1-3 in order of preference. 1st was awarded 3points 2nd 2points and 3rd 1 point.

Over all results was as follows.

Powers Johns Lane : 39

Redbreast CS : 18

Connemara Bog Oak : 10
Tyrconnell 11 Whisky Live : 10

Midleton Barry Crockett : 8
Green Spot : 4
Connemara 18yo Amont : 3
Greenore 18yo : 3
Irishman CS : 3
Greenore 19yo (T2) : 2
Kilbeggan 18yo : 1
Writers Tears CS : 1


Any bottling receiving any votes is still a good mark of confidence
