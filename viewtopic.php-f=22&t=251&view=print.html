An Bhun Abhainn

Louisburg, County Mayo.

Great little pub in Louisburg, with a good pint and quite a decent whiskey selection for a pub in a small town.

Irish wise there's the standard, Paddy, Powers, Jameson, Crested 10, Bushmill and Black Bush.

On top of that, and these are only the ones I can remember there's Greenspot, Redbreast, Bushmills 1608, Tyrconnell, Locke's (I think), Clonmel Peated Malt, and a couple of other.

Scotch wise, the one's I can remember are:

3 Different Bunnahabhainns (as you would expect with the name), the standard 12yo, an independent and the Darach Ur which, by the way, was excellent.

Bowmore 15
Ardbeg 10
A Bruichladdich (forget which one)

At least one Balvenie
A Talisker
A Cardhu
A Glenlivet
Glenkinchie 10

There's another 4 or 5 single malts I can't remember right now and a good selection of Scottish blends.

There's a couple of Bourbons also. (Four roses is one of them).
