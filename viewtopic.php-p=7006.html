No, I wasn't talking about spelling at all. I'm talking about the legal definition of whiskey. "Irish whiskey" has one definition North and South, even though this encompasses two countries and even though this means the UK has two (slightly) different definitions of whiskey in law.

This definitely involved negotiation between the UK and Ireland.
