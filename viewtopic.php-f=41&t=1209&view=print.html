What i can tell you is the Hewitt's brand was introduced in 1964, as a lightly peated blended irish whiskey to compete domestically with scotch. It was later reformulated without the peat and was discontinued in the late 90s/early 00s afaik.

So if your bottle is the older label it is probably late 1960s or 70s.
