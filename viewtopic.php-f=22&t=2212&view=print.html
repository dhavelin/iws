Kilbeggan Distillery Connoisseur Experience

Posted: Sun May 18, 2014 7:40 pm
by IrishWhiskey.Com
Hi folks,

Although not a Connoisseur, I was lucky enough to be invited to the very first Kilbeggan "Connoisseur Experience" last week in which a very small group of us received a personal and exclusive behind the scenes tour of Kilbeggan Distillery by Cooley Global Ambassador for Beam, John Cashman.

It was just terrific fun!

As well as the personal tour, we had some exclusive tastings from the Cooley stable, sampled worts and newly distilled produce, had an audience with the Master Distiller of their small Kilbeggan distillery and even bottled our own personal naggin from the cask.

All followed by a late lunch with John and more exclusive tastings from his own personal collection.

They are going to run roughly one a month for the summer. Further details over in the Events guide on my own site at http://irishwhiskey.com/events/connoiss ... kilbeggan/.

Highly recommended, but bring a driver!

Kind regards,

Stuart
