by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Thu May 07, 2009 8:36 pm

Okay I've decided that it is time for a break away group already

I know there are a few people in Galway that are into Irish Whiskey or even just WHisk(e)y. SO I hope that we can get a few more people to join the Irish Whiskey Society.

So I am hoping to get this going so I won't have to trek up and down to Dublin all the time.

Whether or not join the society it would be nice to be able to meet up with a fwe like mind individuals here in the west.

I have so far been in contact with McCambridge's, Harvest, Neachtain's pub & Freeney's pub. I have posted flyers and hope that I may get some response.

I am also going to advertise on the Galway part of boards.ie

Hopefully in time we may be able to get a nice group going.

I had a good conversation with Luke in McCambridge's and he has hinted that there could well be a few whiskey tasting in store shortly anyway so that is a positive.

So if your interested and Galway based get in contact and lets try and get this going.

This is obviously not going to happen over night and will require some organising so please have patience.
