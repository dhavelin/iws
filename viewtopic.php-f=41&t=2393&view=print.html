Uisce beatha may be interested to know that the owner of Fitzgerald & Co Ltd was a prominent Dublin businessman and politician named Martin Fitzgerald who was born in my home town of Charlestown, Co. Mayo in 1867. He was the last owner of The Freeman's Journal which he purchased in 1919 and which became the unofficial organ of the Irish Free State government. The paper was subsumed into the Irish Independent in 1925. Fitzgerald was appointed as an independent Senator in the inaugural Seanad Eireann from 1922-1927, the year he died. He was also a well-know racehorse owner.
