There is a multitude of tastings that we can put together for Irish WHiskey ... never mind other whisk(e)y

Here area few examples that I could put together but remember these can always be mixed and matched...

A day in the Bog : A starter tasting
Bog standard Irish Blends: (select from) Bushmills White- Jameson-Kilbeggan-Lockes- Paddy- Powers Gold-Tullamore Dew.

Less Well known Blends : (Select from) Clontarf Blend-Michael Collins Blend-Dunphys-Feckin Irish-Merry’s Blend –Murphy’s - Old Dublin- Wild Geese Blend

A step up Tasting : Bushmills 10yo, Connemara 12yo, Jameson 12yo, Knappogue 1995 13yo, Lockes 8yo, Powers 12yo, Tullamore Dew 12yo.

Bushmills HTH Tasting : White Bush-Blackbush-Bushmills 10yo-Bushmills 400Ann-Bushmills 16yo- Bushmills Single Cask- Bushmills 21yo

Connemara HTH Tasting : Connemara, Connemara Cask Strength, Connemara 12yo, Connemara Single Cask 13yo/14yo/15yo, Connemara Single Sherry Cask.

Grain HTH Tasting: Greenore 8yo, Greenore 8yo Ltd Ed, Greenore 15yo Ltd Ed, Adelphi 9yo Shannon Grain, Milroys of Soho 16yo Grain.

Jameson HTH Tasting : Jameson, Jameson 12yo, Jameson 12yo Distillery Reserve, Jameson Gold, Jameson Marconi, Jameson 18yo, Jameson Rarest

Knappogue HTH Tasting: Kanppogue 1991/1992/1993/1994 10yo/1994 12yo/1995 12yo/1995 13yo (Possibly the 1951 to Top it off)

Indy Single Malts unpeated: Brogans Legacy, Clontarf Malt, Michael Collins SM, Irishman, Magilligan 1991, Sainsbury's Single Malt, Signatory 10yo Malt,

Tyrconnell HTH Tasting: Tyrconnell Regular, 13yo/14yo/15yo Single Casks, Maderia/Port/Sherry Casks

Odds & Ends: Henry Downes, Erin Go Bragh Singlr Malt, Hennessey na Geanna, Hewitts Blend, Carlingford Blend, Redbreast Blend, JMR Smooth Sweeter One. Celtic Nations

Old Times versus New: Bushmills 70's v Bushmills, Jameson 70's V Jameson, Paddy 70's V Paddy, Power 70's V Power, Tullamore 70's V Tullamore

Independent Malts Peats : Clonmel 8yo, Erin’s Isle,Magilligan 8yo, James McArthurs, Murray McDavid, McNeills Choice, First Spirits, SMWS 118.1

Cadenheads Peated HTH Tasting: 10yo/12yo/13yo/14yo/15yo/16yo

Superior PPS Tasting: (Select from) Old Comber, Dungourney 1964, Greenspot 10yo Ltd Ed, Jameson 15yo PPS, Kilbeggan 1951, Midleton 25yo, Willie Napier 1945, Redbreast 15yo, Tullamore 1949. However this could easily be 100Euro plus a ticket.


P.S HTH is Head to Head just in case anybody was wondering
