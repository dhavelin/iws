As TWB said 2- is the year, 2012 here. 270 is the Julian day 270/365(27th Sept?). The next, "3" is the bottling plant. That's Waterford here. "1" is Fox & Geese Dublin, "2" is Belfast I think. The final numbers "1283" are the batch number I assume.
