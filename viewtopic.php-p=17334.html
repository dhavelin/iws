Re: Old bushmills whiskey bottle

by TheWhiskeyBro » Thu Sep 19, 2013 10:41 pm

2 US fluid ounces Is about 60ml & 2 imperial fluid ounces is about 57ml both of which are called miniatures.
Bushmills miniatures are quite common.
The date 1784 was on Bushmills bottles up until at least 1972.
Without a picture and based on the information you provided it could date anywhere between 1930-60.
Without a label only worth a $1 or so.
