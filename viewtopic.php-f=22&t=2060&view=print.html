Hey everyone,

These are some whiskey establishments down under;

New distillery in Essendon, Melbourne, Australia: Starward, www.starward.com.au I took a sample bottle to the last tasting and anyone who tasted it thought that it was one of the best whiskeys on the night.

Biggest whiskey bar and school in Australia with between 300 and 500 different bottles of whiskey: Whisky Alement, 270 Russell St, Melbourne. www.whiskyandale.com.au

Shop in Auckland, New Zealand; Shop 11 Elliot Stables, 41 Elliott St, CBD, Auckland. www.whiskyshop.co.nz They even had Green Spot for sale

The New Zealand Whisky Company, 14-16 Harbour St, Oamaru, 9400 NZ Remainder of whiskey from the very last whiskey produced in the last distillery in N.Z.
