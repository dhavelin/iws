June Tasting Event - The Society's Favourite Scotch

Posted: Fri May 25, 2012 12:11 am
by Stephen
Ok, we're going for something different for our June tasting and we're opening up the floor to our members to discuss the their favourite Scotch ....

What we're looking for is a number of volunteers to select a bottle of their favourite scotch and then explain why it is their favourite. So take the favoured dusty bottle off the shelf and drop me an email at stephen dot young at irishwhiskeysociety dot com

Do not post up on the forum which bottle you intend to present, I will collate the different bottles and will select 6 bottles in total that are different enough. We will reimburse the members for their bottles (fair market value, but capped at 50-60 euro, so unfortunately that bottle of Bowmore 64 will have to stay on the shelf)

At the end of the evening we'll vote on who presented the most compelling case for their whisky so you'll have to be at your most convincing ....
