http://www.ebay.co.uk/itm/Jameson-Irish ... 25794d1281

Auction starts at £0.01 but international postage is £500 ,
but there's a reason for this and I quote,

Postage and Packaging.
Unfortunately we are not allowed by our landlord to sell small items from our door.Our nearest post office for posting is over 2 miles away , meaning a 4 mile return journey taking up to 30 minutes by car depending on traffic and then there is car parking to be paid for as well.There is also the time involved in placing the advert,responding to enquiries and packaging the item for safe delivery.Unfortunately i have had to change to a more expensive courier due to an item being broken by my previous courier.This new courier practically garauntees safe delivery but the downside is that they are way more expensive.There is also a .25 p charge per item towards the cost of taking out an insurance policy which covers us for losses caused by for fraudulent buyers. So when we decide the postage and packaging costs we also have to factor in these costs as well.Not to mention the large charges that ebay and paypal impose .When you add all these costs together other people make more money from the sale of the item than i do.When you take all this in to account i find that £4.00 to cover all these costs to be quite reasonable for mainland UK destinations.For non UK destinations i just picked £500 to cover all the other countries in the world.If you are from outside the UK and are interested in bidding on the item,please contact us before you start bidding and we will give you an accurate price for postage to your country.
Please Please read our advert carefully.
