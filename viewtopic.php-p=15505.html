Mars Maltage Komagatake (Shinshu) 10 yo 40%. I'm not too keen on the colour, that deep rich colour of David Dickinson. by bredman » Wed Nov 21, 2012 2:58 am

JohnM wrote:I have a few Mars - although not this particular bottling. They're distilling in a new location, though. Is that right? Some good value in Mars, compared to Karuizawa anyway.


Not exactly John. I have a couple bottles of Malt Of Kagoshima (single casks) which are the old Mars distillery (some of the whisky from that era is sold as Satsuma iirc). They swapped in 1985 to Shinshu factory and afaik they have used that location ever since and the whisky from there is dubbed "Komagatake". They restarted in 2011 after a 10 year mothballing and Dramtastic rates the new make (10 months old) very highly -- both a peated and unpeated style. They were going to distill sooner but there was a story about one of the stills being condemned as "dangerous".

JohnM wrote:What distillery might you compare it to?


I was told to expect a Highland Park. On the first sip i immediately thought Highland Park 12 -- power of suggestion?? I do have a recent HP12 sample somewhere and i'll dig it out tomorrow for a h2h. The original 10 came in a porcelain bottle and is apparently superior. The nose on this one is truly delightful, if they dropped the fake tan and boosted it to 46% it would be up there with the very best there is.
