Hi Tony,
That's a nice bottle to have. They retail for upwards of €1250. That's shop prices. But you'd expect to pay serious money privately too.
I assume the box has the same number as the bottle. I heard of some that don't and that reduces that value.

If I was selling mine I'd try The Celtic Whiskey Shop or The Whiskey Exchange. You could always go the auction route trough Mealy's or whiskyauctions.com the only thing is the commission would eat into you sale price.

You'd never know a collector here may want one.
