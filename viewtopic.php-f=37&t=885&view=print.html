Even though I was not there I have heard that it was a night to remember and some excellent examples were produced for tasting.

Would love to here the full (or as close as you can) story

The line up was

1. Kilbeggan New Spirit 30/9/10 (about 3 days old) from one of the first mashes at Kilbeggan since 1953.
2. Kilbeggan Distillery Reserve Batch 0001 6/10 (June 2010) [L10117]
3. Kilbeggan Blended Irish Whiskey [L10118]
4. Tyrconnell 10yo CWS Single Cask Mallorcan Wine Finish [L10156]
5. Kilbeggan Distilled Peated malt KD09/01 Cask # 8, d. 30/1/2009
6. COOLEY Heavily Peated malt K07/03 Cask # 392, d. 2/5/2007
7. Connemara Peated Single Malt nas [L10150]

Sampling Bottles:

1. Kilbeggan Finest Irish Whiskey Aged 15 Years Old [L10042]
2. Greenore Single Grain Irish Whiskey 15 Years Old [L10033]
3. Tyrconnell Single Malt Irish Whiskey [L10167]
