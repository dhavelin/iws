yes, unfortunately as John has already said without the foil seal that should have been attached it will greatly detract from value. Had it been in tact you could have had a €1000 + bottle on your hands but without the seal it cannot really be authenticated.

Best bet is remove the wax off the top and take it to the Celtic Whiskey Shop and see what they make of if as any buyer would really need to see this in person to give it a good inspection as anything with out a seal is usually viewed with suspicion not matter how authentic the bottle may be. Just an unfortunate situation in your case.

Probably some one would buy it for drinking and take a punt on €100 (Just speculation on my half with the price) as the collectablity is not really there in this case.

Sorry if I am bearer of bad news.

You could also take it to Mitchells and see what they say. if they are happy you may get a few hundred for it
