by IrishWhiskeyChaser » Sat Mar 01, 2014 11:08 am

There was an outer sleeve which elongated the box and the Crystal Tumbler was in a smaller box on top. For some reason Tipperary Crystal rings a bell but could be wrong. I used to have an advertising pic of it but it's stuck on an old computer

However don't get hung up on getting the bits that were attached as they would not have been on the full run of the vintage.

Just an intact box and bottle with certificate is the key and even certificates are optional.

The goal and the prize is the full collection and if you can then sell it you'll probably make a tidy profit.
