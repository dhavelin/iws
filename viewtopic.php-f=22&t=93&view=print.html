Not sure JC, but I was aware of 2 which did not ever go anywhere.

One had a working title of Willbrook for which I came across a Home page yonks ago but there was no info on it. May well of been the Galway one but just a guess. However I think Willbrook is in Co Clare.

The other which seemed more forward in planning was called Coola Mills. This was going to go on a site just 1 km north of Lockes Kilbeggan distillery on the site of a ruined mill on the river brusna. I have a basic whiskey investment prospectus for this but again it never took off the ground. I think they were asking too much for the whiskey futures. Again had a website but alas gone now.

€3,750 per cask of whiskey (Unless the price was out of bond which I very much doubt)

or €1,800 cask if you bought 12 or more.

Not really inviting or maybe the just wanted big investors.
