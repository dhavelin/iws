Re: Kilbeggan 21 year old.

by whiskey nut » Sun Jan 11, 2015 12:58 am

Had the pleasure of tasting this lovely blend at the Irish Whiskey Awards 2014 ceremony a few months ago at the Kilbeggan Distillery.

Beam / Suntory used this event to launch 2 new expressions and John Cashman, Global Brand Ambassador for Beam / Suntory did a very entertaining speech to introduce us to Kilbeggan 21 yo and Connemara 22 yo.

I must admit to not being a peated fan, so the Connemara was lost on me, but the Kilbeggan certainly impressed.

Being a relative newbie to whiskey in the last few years I can't do tasting notes other than it's rich and spicy. The only thing that holds me back is the 125 euro price tag, although The Loop at Dublin Airport has it for 115.
