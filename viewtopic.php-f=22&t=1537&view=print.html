I'm not up to speed with the very latest Dun Leires but i did buy a bunch of the 1st and 2nd batches. Both were excellent value Irish malts and worthy purchases. The Dun Leire is certainly superior to the Sainsbury's Irish Single Malt (Cooley), being more complex and richer. But it must be said they are similar in profile and instantly recognisable as siblings.
