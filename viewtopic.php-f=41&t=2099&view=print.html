Done Deal and whiskey

Posted: Fri Jan 10, 2014 7:03 pm
by mawhinney
It occurred to me that Done Deal , http://www.donedeal.ie, might be explored to determine its potential for enabling the disposal and acquisition, aka selling and buying, of collectible whiskey among enthusiasts. It is a facility that could avoid some of the delays and hassle associated with whiskey auctions. And, of course,this IWS Forum is not a trading site.
A thought is fruitless without action, and so to this end I have have launched the boat with the listing of a naggin bottle of Green Spot,old-style label, screw top.
I shall report on response and, meanwhile, invite others to participate to help establish a whiskey stream in the Collectibles section of Done Deal.

Hi Ken,
They may remove the ad, it breaches their terms and conditions, sale of alcohol.

I have contacted various people who have posted whiskey for sale and notice the ads are removed. When I ring again to see of the bottle is gone they tell me he ad was removed by Donedeal. I seen your ad yesterday and hopefully all goes well. If you post 'whiskey for sale', it's usually removed quick enough.

You can't sell on eBay here or the UK either. Adverts.ie are strict too.

The forum is a good spot for people who want to sell, as long as they are not openly discussing price and personal details. I managed to get a couple of gems over the years.

Best wishes with the Green Spot.
