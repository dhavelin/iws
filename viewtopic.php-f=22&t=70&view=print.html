Scanlan´s Tavern Gernika Bizkaia(Euskadi)Spain.

Failte and welcome to the Basque Countrys Irish gem,nestled snugly in the market town of Gernika located on the rugged coastal route to Santiago.An award winning Whiskey bar Irish owned,boasting the worlds largest"Pub" collection of Irish Whiskeys (150 approx) and is dedicated to Irish Expressions.Only selling Irish they have 30 to sell by the Dram.Regular bespoke tastings are run for groups and companies and gourmet tour groups.
Their are direct flights from Dublin to Bilbao so dont be shy to check out what is a Mecca for an Irish Whiskey afficanado,Slainte !!
Blogs: scanlanstavern.blogspot.com & catadewhiskeyenspain.blogspot.com
