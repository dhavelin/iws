Don't have much info but these were made for the US market. Empty ones crop regular enough but I have not seen a full one in a while.

The state of the contents really depends on how good or bad the cork is and how high the fill level is.

It won't hard you though no matter how it is but if it has been exposed to the air it may have gone a bit flat or dull in taste.
