Hello fine people of Irish Whiskey Society,
I've been working on a tool for the past 6 months that I finally feel comfortable enough to share. It helps you sort, search, and find the right bottle of alcohol or cocktail based on your drinking preferences. I recently rebuilt the Irish Whiskey content and thought you guys might like it.

This tool comes in the form of an android app http://bit.ly/utlforums and as a website http://bit.ly/1EtUkSy

What does it do?
Search across a variety of different alcohol types -- whiskey, beer, vodka, tequila, gin, brandy, liqueurs, and more.
Gain valuable insights about the value of a bottle based on its characteristics.
Utilize my "Smart Rating" to choose the best bottle at a glance.
Compare bottles side-by-side based on price, professional reviews, tasting notes, availability, and more.
Understand every aspect of the spirit, from production to taste
Create your own visualizations using a vast library of data.
Review bottles you have tried and share your expertise with others in the community.
Add new bottles from your local brewery or distillery.
This app also contains a vast cocktail and drinking games database with more than 16,000 cocktails and 200 drinking games.
Search for cocktails based on ingredients you already have at home.
Create and add your own cocktails and drinking games to share with your friends.

Add your own images and reviews and share them.
