Postage and Packaging.
Unfortunately we are not allowed by our landlord to sell small items from our door.Our nearest post office for posting is over 2 miles away , meaning a 4 mile return journey taking up to 30 minutes by car depending on traffic and then there is car parking to be paid for as well.There is also the time involved in placing the advert,responding to enquiries and packaging the item for safe delivery.Unfortunately i have had to change to a more expensive courier due to an item being broken by my previous courier.This new courier practically garauntees safe delivery but the downside is that they are way more expensive.There is also a .25 p charge per item towards the cost of taking out an insurance policy which covers us for losses caused by for fraudulent buyers.
