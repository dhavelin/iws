Re: Irish Mist

by TheWhiskeyBro » Sun Jun 14, 2009 12:50 pm

Irish Mist does exactly what it says on the tin. A sweet liquer with honey and spices. The original concoction saved the Tullamore Dew brand due to its success in the export market particularly the US. The story is quite interesting, which is for another day, but basically they were searching for a recipe and after searching through historical texts without finding a successful formula, someone from Tullamore met a gentleman from Austria who had an old Irish recipe in his possession, the rest is history. Essentially it is an after dinner liquer, but a bit sweet for todays palette, more commonly uset in cocktails nowadays.
